ذيم ذين ر

1. ⇒ ذين ⇒ ذان

ذَانَهُ, [aor. يَذِينُ, inf. n., app., ذَيْنٌ,] He blamed, or found fault with, him, or it; like ذَامَهُ. (IAạr, T.)

ذَيْنٌ

ذَيْنٌ, (M, TA,) incorrectly said in the Ḳ to be ذِيْنٌ, with kesr, (TA,) A vice, fault, defect, or the like, (M, Ḳ, TA;) as also ذَانٌ [which belongs to art. ذون]. (M.)

مُذَانٌ

مُذَانٌ a dial. var. of مُذَالٌ. (M.) [See the latter in art. ذيل.]
