ذلك ذلى ذم

1. ⇒ ذلى

ذَلَى الرُّطَبَ, like سَعَى, (Ḳ,) [i. e.,] aor. ـَ {يَذْلَىُ}, inf. n. ذَلْىٌ, (TA,) He gathered the fresh ripe dates: (Ḳ:) so in the copies of the Ḳ; in which is added, فَٱنْذَلَى مَعَهُ: but what we find in the Tekmileh is this: ظلّ يذلى الرطب اى يجنيه فينذلى معه: and يذلى is written as [the aor. of] a quadriliteral [i. e. as the aor. of اذلى, for it is without a sheddeh]: (TA:) [here, however, فينذلى is evidently, in my opinion, a mistranscription for فَيَتَدَلَّى; and the right reading and rendering I therefore hold to be as follows: ظَلَّ يُذْلِى↓ الرُّطَبَ, or perhaps يَذْلَى, means He continued gathering the fresh ripe dates, they hanging down with him: for the gatherer laying hold upon the raceme, it hangs down with his weight. In the TḲ, this passage in the TA has been misunderstood and misrepresented, as though it meant that أَذْلَى مَعَهُ signifies “he gathered with him.”]

4. ⇒ اذلى

see the preceding paragraph {1}.

5. ⇒ تذلّى

تذلّى i. q. تذلّل [He became lowly, humble, or submissive; or he lowered, humbled, or submitted, himself]: (T, Ḳ:) the latter verb is the original: the former being like تظنّى, originally تظنّن. (T.) [See also the next paragraph {12}.]

12. ⇒ اذلولى

اِذْلَوْلَى, (T, Ṣ, M, Ḳ,) inf. n. اِذْلِيلَآءٌ, (Ṣ,) He went away hiding himself; stole away secretly. (T, Ṣ, M, Ḳ.)

Root: ذلى - Entry: 12. ― Signification: A2

He hastened, made haste, sped, or went quickly; (TA;) [like ادلولى;] and (TA) he did so in fear lest a thing should escape him. (T, TA.) And اذلولى فَذَهَبَ He went back, or away, running quickly. (T.)

Root: ذلى - Entry: 12. ― Signification: A3

He was, or became, easy, tractable, submissive, or manageable. (M, Ḳ.) [See also 5.]

Root: ذلى - Entry: 12. ― Signification: A4

He (a man) was, or became, broken-hearted. (T, Ḳ.)

Root: ذلى - Entry: 12. ― Signification: A5

It (the ذَكَر) stood in a lax state. (T, Ḳ.)

ذَلَوْلًى

ذَلَوْلًى, [in copies of the Ḳ ذَلَولَى,] applied to a man, i. q. مُذْلَوْلٍ [part. n. of 12, q. v.]: (Ḳ, TA: [in some copies of the Ḳ مَذْلُولٌ:]) of the measure فَعَوْعَلٌ; or, as some say, فَعَلْعَلٌ. (TA.)

مُذْلَوْلٍ

رِشَآءٌ مُذْلَوْلٍ [A well-rope] unsteady; or moving about, or to and fro, or from side to side. (T.)
