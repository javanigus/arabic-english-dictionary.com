ذور ذوف ذوق

1. ⇒ ذوف ⇒ ذاف

ذَافَ, (M, Ḳ,) aor. يَذُوفُ, (M,) inf. n. ذَوْفٌ, He walked with short steps, and in a straddling manner. (M, Ḳ.)

Root: ذوف - Entry: 1. ＝ Dissociation: B

And ذُفْتُ is a dial. var. of ذُفْتُ, signifying I mixed [medicine, &c.] (M.)

ذُوفَانٌ

ذُوفَانٌ [like ذُؤْفَانٌ, &c.] Poison: (Ḳ:) or poison made into a confection: or deadly poison: like ذِيفَانٌ [&c.]. (M.)
