اط اطر اطم

1. ⇒ أطر

أَطَرَهُ, aor. ـِ {يَأْطِرُ} (Ṣ, Mṣb, Ḳ) and ـُ {يَأْطُرُ}, (Ḳ,) inf. n. أَطْرٌ; (Ṣ, Mṣb, Ḳ;) andأطّرهُ↓ inf. n. تَأْطِيرٌ; (Ḳ;) He bent it, or curved it; (Ṣ, Mṣb, Ḳ, &c.;) namely, a bow, (Ṣ, A,) and a twig, or the like: (A:) he laid hold upon one of its two extremities, and curved it: he bent it, or curved it; namely, anything; عَلَى شَّىْءٍ upon a thing: and the latter verb, [or both,] he bent it into the form of a hoop, bringing its two extremities together. (TA.)

Root: اطر - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A2

It is said of Adam, كَانَ طُوَالًا فَأَطَّرَهُ↓ ٱللّٰهُ He was tall, and God bent him, and diminished his height. (TA.)

Root: اطر - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A3

And one says, أَخَذَ عَلَى يَدَىِ الظَّالِمِ وَأَطَرَهُ عَلَى الحَقِّ ‡ [He laid hold upon the two hands, or arms, of the wrongdoer, or prevented, restrained, or withheld, him from doing that which he desired,] and bent him to [conformity with] what was right. (AA, from a trad.) And أَطَرْتَ فُلَانًا عَلَى مَوَدَّتِكَ ‡ [Thou hast bent such a one to love thee]. (A.)

Root: اطر - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A4

أَطَرَ السَّهْمَ (Ṣ, Ḳ,) aor. ـِ {يَأْطِرُ} and ـُ {يَأْطُرُ}, (Ḳ,) inf. n. as above, (Ṣ, Ḳ,) He wound an أُطْرَة upon the arrow. (Ṣ, Ḳ.)

Root: اطر - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A5

أَطَرَ البَيْتَ, (TḲ,) inf. n. as above, (Ḳ,) He made an إِطَار, which is a thing resembling a zone or belt, to the tent or house. (Ḳ, TḲ.)

2. ⇒ أطّر

see 1, in two places.

Root: اطر - Entry: 2. ＝ Dissociation: B

أَطَّرَتْ, inf. n. تَأْطِيرٌ She (a girl, IAạr) remained in the house, or tent, of her father, some time, or long, (IAạr, Ḳ,) without marrying. (IAạr.) [See also 5.]

5. ⇒ تأطّر

تأطّر It (a spear) bent: (Ṣ, Ḳ:) it (a thing) became crooked, curved, or bent; as alsoانأطر↓: (Ḳ, TA:) it became bent into the form of a hoop, its two extremities being brought together. (TA.)

Root: اطر - Entry: 5. ― Signification: A2

تَأَطَّرَتْ She affected a bending of her person, body, or limbs, in her gait. (A.)

Root: اطر - Entry: 5. ＝ Dissociation: B

He confined himself (Ḳ, TA) in a place. (TA.)

Root: اطر - Entry: 5. ― Signification: B2

تَأَطَّرَتْ She (a woman) remained, or stayed, in her house, or tent; (Ṣ, Ḳ;) she kept to it. (TA.) [See also 2.]

7. ⇒ انأطر

see 5.

أَطْرٌ

أَطْرٌ The place of curvature (مُنْحَنَى) of a bow, and of a cloud: (Ḳ, TA:) an inf. n. used as a subst., and, being so used, admitting the dual form: or the bent, or curved, part of the extremity of a bow; to which Tarafeh likens the curving of the ribs of a she-camel: (TA:) and what resembles a curvature, seen in the clouds: an inf. n. in the sense of a pass. part. n. (Skr, TA.)

أُطْرَةٌ

أُطْرَةٌ The sinew that is wound immediately above the notch of an arrow; (Ṣ, Ḳ;) as alsoإِطَارٌ↓. (Ḳ.)

Root: اطر - Entry: أُطْرَةٌ ― Signification: A2

The edge of the glans of the penis; (Ḳ,* TA;) as also↓ the latter word. (Ḳ, TA.)

Root: اطر - Entry: أُطْرَةٌ ― Signification: A3

The flesh surrounding the nail: (Ḳ:) pl.أُطَرٌ and إِطَارٌ. (TA.)

Root: اطر - Entry: أُطْرَةٌ ― Signification: A4

A mixture of ashes and blood with which a fracture in a cooking-pot is smeared (Ṣ, Ḳ) and repaired. (TA.)

إِطَارٌ

إِطَارٌ Anything that surrounds another thing: (Ṣ, A, Mṣb, Ḳ:) as the hoop of a tambourine, (A, Mgh, TA,) and of a sieve. (Ṣ, A, Mgh, Ḳ.)

Root: اطر - Entry: إِطَارٌ ― Signification: A2

A ring of hair surrounding the head, the middle of it being bald. (TA.)

Root: اطر - Entry: إِطَارٌ ― Signification: A3

The branches of a vine, bent, or wreathed, so as to form a covering over-head. (Ḳ.)

Root: اطر - Entry: إِطَارٌ ― Signification: A4

See also أُطْرَةٌ, in two places.

Root: اطر - Entry: إِطَارٌ ― Signification: A5

إِطَارُ الحَافِرِ The part of the hoof of a horse or the like which surrounds, or extends around, the أَشْعَر [q.v.]. (Ṣ.)

Root: اطر - Entry: إِطَارٌ ― Signification: A6

إِطَارُ الشَّفَةِ (Ṣ, Ḳ, &c.) ‡ The part, (A,) or flesh, (Mṣb,) surrounding the lip: (A, Mṣb:) or the part that separates between the lip and the hairs of the mustache: (Ḳ:) or the edge of the upper lip, between the lip itself and the parts where the hair grows: (IAth:) or the rising edge, or ridge, between the part where the mustache is clipped and the lip, intermixing with the mouth. (AʼObeyd.) The Muslim should clip his mustache so that this part shall appear. (Mṣb, TA.)

Root: اطر - Entry: إِطَارٌ ― Signification: A7

إِطَارُ بَيْتٍ A thing resembling a zone, or belt, of a tent or house. (Ḳ.)

Root: اطر - Entry: إِطَارٌ ― Signification: A8

إِطَارِ مِنَ النَّاسِ ‡ A ring, or circle, of men. (Ḳ.) One says, هُمْ إِطَارٌ لِبَنِى فُلَانٍ ‡ They have alighted and taken up their abode [so that they form a ring] around the sons of such a one. (A, Mṣb.)

أَطِيرٌ

أَطِيرٌ A sin; a crime; an offence. (Ṣ, Ḳ.) One says, أَخَذَنِى بِأَطِيرِ غَيْرِى He punished me for the sin, crime, or offence, of another than myself. (Ṣ.)

مَأْطُورَةٌ

مَأْطُورَةٌ A bow. (A.)

Root: اطر - Entry: مَأْطُورَةٌ ― Signification: A2

A milking-vessel of skin (عُلْبَةٌ) for the head of which a twig is bent into the form of a hoop, and put round, after which its lip is covered; (Ḳ, TA;) or, sometimes, the edges of the skin of the علبة are folded upon the hoop-formed twig, and dry upon it. (TA.)
