كمى كن كنب

كِنٌّ

كِنٌّ A place of retreat or concealment; such as a cave, and an excavated house or chamber: (Beyd, xvi. 83:) see بَرٌّ.

Root: كن - Entry: كِنٌّ ― Signification: A2

كِنٌّ The shelter of a wall: see دِفْءٌ.

Root: كن - Entry: كِنٌّ ― Signification: A3

A thing that serves for veiling, covering, or protecting. (Ṣ,* Mṣb,* Ḳ.) See ظِلٌّ, where I have thus rendered it.

كُنَّةٌ

كُنَّةٌ: see طَنَفٌ.

كِنَانَةٌ

كِنَانَةٌ: see جَعْبَةٌ.

كَانُونٌ

كَانُونٌ A fire-place; a place in which fire is lighted. (Ṣ, Ḳ.)

مَكْنُونُ

مَكْنُونُ الفَائِلِ: see a verse of El-Aạshà in art. فيل.

مُسْتَكِنٌّ

فاعل مُسْتَكِنٌّ: see مُسْتَقِنٌ.
