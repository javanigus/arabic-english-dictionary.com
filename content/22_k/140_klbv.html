كلب كلبث كلت

كَلْبَثٌ

كَلْبَثٌ and كُلَابِثٌ A hard and strong man. (IDrd, L.)

Root: كلبث - Entry: كَلْبَثٌ ― Signification: A2

Also, and كُلْبُثٌ and كُلَبِثٌ, Niggardly, or stingy, and contracted [in disposition]. (Ḳ.) [See also كُنْبُثٌ.]
