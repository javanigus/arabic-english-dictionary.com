كتد كتف كتل

كَتِفٌ

كَتِفٌ [The shoulder-blade;] a wide bone behind the shoulder-joint. (Mgh.)

Root: كتف - Entry: كَتِفٌ ― Signification: A2

[Hence, The shoulder itself.] See طُرَّةٌ and مُؤُرَّبٌ.

كُتْفاَنٌ

كُتْفاَنٌ, as an epithet applied to the locust, see in TA, voce مُسَيَّحٌ. See also جَرَادٌ.

كِتَافٌ

كِتَافٌ of a زَبِيل: see حَتِىٌّ.

كَتِيفَةٌ

كَتِيفَةٌ i. q. ضَبَّةٌ A broad piece of iron. A poet speaks of a wooden vessel of which a fracture is mended with a كتيفة. (Ṣ.)
