كز كزب كزبر

كُزْبٌ

كُزْبٌ i. q. كُسْبٌ. (Ḳ.)

Root: كزب - Entry: كُزْبٌ ― Signification: A2

[Coll. gen. n., A kind of] hard trees. (Ḳ.)

كَزَبٌ

كَزَبٌ Smallness and contraction of the مُشْط (or metatarsal bones) of the foot; which is a defect. (Ḳ.)

كَوْزَبٌ

كَوْزَبٌ Avaricious, or niggardly, and narrowminded. (Ḳ.)

مَكْزُوبَةٌ

مَكْزُوبَةٌ i. q. خِلَاسِيَّة in colour; i. e., between black and white. (Ḳ.)
