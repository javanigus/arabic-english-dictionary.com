كرتب كرتح كرث

Q. 1. ⇒ كرتح

كَرْتَحَ, inf. n. كَرْتَحَةٌ, He (a short man) ran with short steps, and quickly; as also كَرْدَحَ. (Ṣ, art. كردح.)

Root: كرتح - Entry: Q. 1. ― Signification: A2

Also, (TA,) andتَكَرْتَحَ↓, (Ḳ,) He went quickly in his walk. (Ḳ, TA.)
