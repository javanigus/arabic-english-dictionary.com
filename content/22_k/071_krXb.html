كرش كرشب كرص

كِرْشَبٌّ

كِرْشَبٌّ i. q. قِرْشَبٌّ: (Ḳ:) or the former signifies Advanced in years, and hard, gross, or coarse: and the latter, a great eater, or voracious. (T.) The ك is said to be substituted for ق, or viciously pronounced for the latter letter. (MF.)
