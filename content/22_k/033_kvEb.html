كثر كثعب كثنب

كَثْعَبٌ

كَثْعَبٌ (as also كَعْثَبٌ, TA,) A woman having a large pubes, (Ḳ,) or pudendum. (TA.)

كَثْعَبٌ

رَكَبٌ كَثْعَبٌ (as also كَعْثَبٌ, TA,) A large, (and full, and prominent, TA,) pubes, (Ḳ,) or pudendum. (TA.)
