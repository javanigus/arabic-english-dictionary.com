كعثب كعدب كعسب

كَعْدَبٌ

كَعْدَبٌ and كَعْدَبَةٌ A base, unmanly, person. (Ḳ.)

كُعْدُبَةٌ

كُعْدُبَةٌ Bubbles that float upon the surface of water; (Ḳ;) resulting from rain: (TA:) a bubble floating upon the surface of water, &c. (TA): or a spider's web: (AA:)

Root: كعدب - Entry: كُعْدُبَةٌ ― Signification: A2

Also, Stones. (TA.)

Root: كعدب - Entry: كُعْدُبَةٌ ― Signification: A3

All these significations are also assigned to جُعْدُبَةٌ. (TA, art. جعدب, q. v.)
