كيص كيف كيل

2. ⇒ كيّف

كَيَّفْتُهُ فَتَكَيَّفَ, used by the Muslim theologians, (Ḳ, TA,) verbs derived from كَيْفَ, (TA,) [signifying I specified by the ascription of its quality and it became so specified,] are formed in accordance with analogy, not heard from the Arabs. (Ḳ,* TA.)

5. ⇒ تكيّف

تَكَيَّفَ: see what next precedes {2}.

كَيْفِيَّةٌ

كَيْفِيَّةٌ Quality as answering to “how?”; mode, or manner, of being.

كَيْفُوفِيَّةٌ

كَيْفُوفِيَّةٌ for كَيْفِيَّةٌ: see تَيْسِيَّةٌ.
