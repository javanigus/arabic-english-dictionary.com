كنب كنبت كنبث

Q. 2. ⇒ تكنبت

تَكَنْبَتَ He (a man) became contracted [in disposition; or niggardly, or stingy]. (L, as from IDrd.) But see art. كنبث. (TA.)

كُنْبُبٌ

كُنْبُبٌ, (or this should be كُنْثُبٌ, TA) A hard, strong, robust, man. (L.) But see كُنْبُثٌ. (TA.)

Root: كنبت - Entry: كُنْبُبٌ ― Signification: A2

Also, and كُنَابِتٌ, A man contracted [in disposition]; niggardly, or stingy. (L.) But see art. كنبث. (TA.)
