كعب كعبر كعت

كُعْبُرَةٌ

كُعْبُرَةٌ andكُعْبُورَةٌ↓ Anything compact (Ḳ, TA) and round: (TA:) and the latter, i. q. عُقْدَةٌ [meaning a knot, or a knob]: (TA:) [pl. of the latter كَعَابِيرُ: see خِرْوٌ: see also أَخْرَمُ].

Root: كعبر - Entry: كُعْبُرَةٌ ― Signification: A2

Also The seed-vessel [i. e. siliqua, or pod,] of the radish and some other plants. (IAạr, TA voce إِسْحَارٌّ.)

كُعْبُوْرَةٌ

كُعْبُوْرَةٌ: see كُعْبُرَةٌ.
