كدش كدم كذ

1. ⇒ كدم

كَدَمَ He bit with the fore part of the mouth. (Ṣ, Mṣb, Ḳ.)

6. ⇒ تكادم

تَكَادُمُ الحُمُرِ [The wild asses' biting one another with the fore part of the mouth]. (TA in art. سحج.) See also تَسَانَّت.
