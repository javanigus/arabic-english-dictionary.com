كهل كو كوأ

كُوَّةٌ

كُوَّةٌ and كَوَّةٌ (Ṣ, Mṣb, Ḳ) and كَوٌّ (Ḳ) A hole, or perforation. or an aperture, (Ṣ, Mgh, Mṣb, Ḳ,) in a wall (Mṣb, Ḳ) or chamber; (Ṣ, Mgh;) [a mural aperture;] or the first and second, a small one; and the third, a large one (Ḳ.) See also مِشْكَاةٌ.
