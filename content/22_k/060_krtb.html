Q. 2. ⇒ تكرتب

تكرتب عَلَيْنَا i. q. تقلّب (He turned over upon us or turned against us:) accord. to the Ḳ: but accord. to the L and other lexicons, i. q. تغلّب (He got possession of us, or obtained the mastery over us, by force). (TA.)
