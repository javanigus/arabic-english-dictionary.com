كرب كربح كربس

Q. 1. ⇒ كربح

كَرْبَحَ He prostrated another: or, [evidently a mistake for and,] inf. n. كَرْبَحَةٌ, he ran heavily; (Ḳ;) as also كَرْمَحَ: (TA:) and he ran at a slower pace than that termed كَرْدَحَةٌ, (Ḳ,) or كَرْدَمَةٌ, which is a pace of the ass and mule only. (L.)
