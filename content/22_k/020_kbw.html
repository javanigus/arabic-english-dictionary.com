كَبَا

كَبَا: said of a horse: see above, art. حَنَذَ, p. 656 b.

Root: كبو - Entry: كَبَا ― Signification: A2

See also a phrase voce سَلَّةٌ.

Root: كبو - Entry: كَبَا ― Signification: A3

كَبَا He fell upon his face: (Ḳ, TA:) or so كَبَا لِوَجْهِهِ: (Ṣ, TA:) and كَباَ also signifies عَثَرَ [he stumbled, or tripped]. (TA.)
