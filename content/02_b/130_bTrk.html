بطرق بطرك بطش

بَطْرَكٌ

بَطْرَكٌ and بِطَرْكٌ i. q. بِطْرِيقٌ, (Aṣ, Ḳ,) i. e. A leader of the Christians: (TA:) or the chief of the Magians: (Ḳ:) [in the present day, the former is applied to a Patriarch of a Christian church; as alsoبِطْرِيكٌ↓: (see جَاثَلِيقٌ:) pl. بَطَارِكَةٌ and بَطَارِيكُ]: adventitious; not Arabic. (Az, TA.)

[بَطْرَكِىٌّ]

[بَطْرَكِىٌّ Patriarchal; i. e. of, or belonging to, or relating to, a Patriarch of a Christian church; as alsoبِطْرِيكِىٌّ↓: both modern terms.]

[بَطْرَكِيَّةٌ]

[بَطْرَكِيَّةٌ A patriarchate; i. e. the office, or jurisdiction, of a Patriarch of a Christian church; as alsoبِطْرِيكِيَّةٌ↓: both modern terms.]
