فدح فدخ فدر

1. ⇒ فدخ

فَدَخَ رَأْسَهُ, aor. ـَ {يَفْدَخُ}, (Ḳ, TA,) inf. n. فَدْخٌ, (TA,) He broke his head with a stone: (Ḳ, TA:) and فَدَخْتُ الشَّىْءَ I broke the thing: (TA:) [but] the verb is not used except in relation to a thing in which is moisture. (Ḳ, TA.)
