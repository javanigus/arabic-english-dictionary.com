فوق فول فولاذ

فُولٌ / فُولَةٌ

فُولٌ [Beans; or the bean; faba sativa of Jussieu; vicia faba of Linnæus; i. e.] i. q. بَاقِلًّى [q. v.]: (T, IF, Ṣ, Mṣb:) or i. q. حِمَّص [i. e. chick-peas; or the chick-pea]: (so in one of my copies of the Ṣ: [in most of the copies of the Ṣ, this word is mentioned at the end of art. فيل; but in the older of my copies, before that art.:]) or a certain grain, or seed, (حَبّ,) like the حِمَّص: but with the people of Syria [and of Egypt], i. q. بَاقِلًّى: or (as some say, M) specially the dry [thereof]: n. un. with ة {فُولَةٌ}: (M, Ḳ:) mentioned by Sb. (M.)

فَوَّالٌ

فَوَّالٌ A seller of فُول. (TA.)
