فيد فيروزج فيش

فِيرُوزَجٌ

فِيرُوزَجٌ [an arabicized word from the Pers. فِيرُوزَهُ or پِيرُوزَهُ The turquoise;] a well-known kind of stone. (TA.)

Root: فيروزج - Entry: فِيرُوزَجٌ ― Signification: A2

And A certain sort of dye [probably of the colour of the stone so called]. (TA.)
