فيح فيخ فيد

1. ⇒ فيخ ⇒ فاخ

فَاخَتِ الرِّيحُ, aor. تَفِيخُ, inf. n. فَيْخٌ and فَيَخَانٌ: see 1 in art. فوخ. Accord. to Kr and the Ḳ, فَيْخٌ signifies A spreading, or becoming diffused; like فِيْحٌ: (TA:) but ISd expresses doubt of the correctness of this: (L, TA:) it is [said to be] an inf. n. in this sense. (TḲ.)

Root: فيخ - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A2

See also 4.

2. ⇒ فيّخ

فيّخ العَجِينَ He made the dough like the سُكُرُّجَة [or فَيْخَة (q. v.) in form]. (TA.)

4. ⇒ افيخ ⇒ افاخ

افاخ, (L,) inf. n. إِفَاخَةٌ, (L, Ḳ,) He (a man, L) emitted wind from the anus, with a sound; (L, Ḳ;) as alsoفَاخَ↓: (L:) or he voided excrement with an emission of wind. (L, Ḳ.) [See also art. فوخ.]

Root: فيخ - Entry: 4. ― Signification: A2

افاخ, said of a man, signifies also سُقِطَ فِى يَدِهِ [i. e. He repented of what he had done;, &c.]. (Ḳ.)

Root: فيخ - Entry: 4. ― Signification: A3

And افاخ مِنْ فُلَانٍ He (a man, TA) turned away from, avoided, shunned, and left, such a one; syn. صَدّ عَنْهُ. (Ḳ.)

فِيْخَةٌ

فِيْخَةٌ A state of dilatation of the place of exit of the urine. (IAạr, Ḳ.)

Root: فيخ - Entry: فِيْخَةٌ ― Signification: A2

And A tangled, luxuriant, or dense, and abundant, state of herbage. (Ḳ.)

Root: فيخ - Entry: فِيْخَةٌ ― Signification: A3

And Vehemence, or intenseness, of heat. (Ḳ, TA.)

Root: فيخ - Entry: فِيْخَةٌ ＝ Dissociation: B

And A [kind of small saucer, or cup, such as is called] سُكُرُّجَة [q. v.]: (Ḳ:) pl. فَيْخَاتٌ. (O and Ḳ voce شُفَارِجٌ.)
