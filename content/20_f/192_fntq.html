فن فنتق فنجان

فُنْتُقٌ

فُنْتُقٌ A خَان of the way or road; (Ibn-ʼAbbád, O, Ḳ;) a dial. var. of فُنْدُقٌ [q. v.]; disapproved by El-Khafájee; but heard by Fr from a desertArab of Kudá'ah, as meaning a فُنْدُق, i. e. خَان. (TA.)
