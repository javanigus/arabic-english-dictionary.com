خسو خسى خش

6. ⇒ تخاسى

التَّخَاسِى The throwing of pebbles, one at another. (Ḳ.) You say, تَخَاسَتْ قَوَائِمُ الدَّابَّةِ بِالحَصَا The legs of the beast threw the pebbles, one at another. (TA.) [See also 3 in art. خسأ.]

خَسِىٌّ

خَسِىٌّ The like of a [garment of the kind called] كِسَآء, or of a [tent such as is called] خِبَآء, woven of wool. (JK, Ḳ.)
