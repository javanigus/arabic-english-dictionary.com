Q. 1. ⇒ ختعر

خَتْعَرَ, inf. n. خَتْعَرَةٌ, It passed away, and came to nought: (Ḳ: but only the inf. n. is there mentioned:) said of the mirage. (Kr.)

خَيْتَعُورٌ

خَيْتَعُورٌ Anything that does not remain in one state; and that passes away, and comes to nought; (Ṣ, Ḳ:) or that has no real existence. (IAth.)

Root: ختعر - Entry: خَيْتَعُورٌ ― Signification: A2

The mirage; syn. سَرَابٌ: (Ṣ, Ḳ:) or what remains of the latter part of the mirage, when it becomes dispersed, and delays not to pass away and come to nought. (Kr, L.)

Root: ختعر - Entry: خَيْتَعُورٌ ― Signification: A3

[Gossamer; i. e.] what descends from the air in a time of intense heat, like spiders' webs; (Ṣ;) a thing like spiders' webs, which appears in a time of heat, (Ḳ,) descending from the sky, (TA,) resembling threads, (Ḳ,) or white threads, (TA,) in the air. (Ḳ.) [See لُعَابُ الشَّمْسِ, in art. لعب.]

Root: ختعر - Entry: خَيْتَعُورٌ ― Signification: A4

The present world or life. (Ḳ.)

Root: ختعر - Entry: خَيْتَعُورٌ ― Signification: A5

The devil: (Fr, Ḳ:) or the devil of the 'Akabeh, called أَزَبُّ العَقَبَةِ (IAth.)

Root: ختعر - Entry: خَيْتَعُورٌ ― Signification: A6

The [imaginary creature called] غُول: (Ṣ, Ḳ:) because it changes its appearance. (TA.)

Root: ختعر - Entry: خَيْتَعُورٌ ― Signification: A7

A certain insect, (Ḳ,) of a black colour, (TA,) that is upon the surface of water, and that does not remain in one place (Ḳ) save as long as the time of the winking of an eye. (TA.)

Root: ختعر - Entry: خَيْتَعُورٌ ― Signification: A8

A perfidious, or an unfaithful, man. (TA.)

Root: ختعر - Entry: خَيْتَعُورٌ ― Signification: A9

A woman whose affection does not last: (TA:) a woman evil in disposition: (Ḳ:) likened to a غُول inasmuch as her love does not last. (TA.)

Root: ختعر - Entry: خَيْتَعُورٌ ― Signification: A10

The wolf: (Ṣ, Ḳ:) because of his unfaithfulness. (TA.)

Root: ختعر - Entry: خَيْتَعُورٌ ― Signification: A11

The lion: (Ḳ:) because of his perfidiousness. (TA.)

Root: ختعر - Entry: خَيْتَعُورٌ ― Signification: A12

A calamity; syn. دَاهِيَةٌ. (Ṣ, Ḳ.)

Root: ختعر - Entry: خَيْتَعُورٌ ― Signification: A13

نَوًى خَيْتَعُورٌ [A point towards which one journeys] that is not in a right direction; syn. اَلَّتِى لَا تَسْتَقِيمُ: (L:) or far distant. (Ḳ.)
