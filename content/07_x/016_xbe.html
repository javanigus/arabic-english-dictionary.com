خِبَآءٌ

خِبَآءٌ A kind of structure; (Ḳ;) [i. e.] one of the بُيُوت [or kinds of tents] of the Arabs, (IAth, TA,) peculiarly of wool, (Yaạḳoob, Th,) or of camels' fur, or of wool, (IAạr, Ṣ, IAth, Ḳ,) or of [goats'] hair, (Ḳ,) or not of [goats'] hair, (Ṣ,) [except in cold countries and in fertile regions, where the goats have abundant hair, for the goats of the Arabs of the desert have short hair, not long enough to be spun, (see بَيْتٌ,)] smaller than the مِظَلَّة, (IAạr,) upon two poles, or three; what is above this kind being termed بَيْتٌ; (Ṣ;) or a tent having one pole; that which has more than one pole being termed بَيْتٌ: (AZ, TA in art. ربع:) pl. أَخْبِيَةٌ, (Ṣ, TA,) without ء: (TA:) most of the lexicologists hold that its radical letters are خبى. (TA in art. خبأ, q. v.)

Root: خبى - Entry: خِبَآءٌ ― Signification: A2

Sometimes, † A dwelling, such as is in a city. (TA, from a trad.)

Root: خبى - Entry: خِبَآءٌ ― Signification: A3

Also ‡ A receptacle for oil or ointment. (Ḳ, TA.)

Root: خبى - Entry: خِبَآءٌ ― Signification: A4

And ‡ The calyx or a flower. (TA.)

Root: خبى - Entry: خِبَآءٌ ― Signification: A5

And ‡ The husk of a grain of wheat, and of a grain of barley, in the ear. (Ḳ, TA.)

Root: خبى - Entry: خِبَآءٌ ― Signification: A6

And الخِبَآءُ signifies ‡ Certain round stars, [or stars in a circle,] (Ḳ, TA,) forming one of the Mansions of the Moon, [namely, the Twenty-fifth,] also called الأَخْبِيَةُ [or سَعْدُ الأَخْبِيَةِ: see سَعْدٌ]. (TA.)
