وشق وشك وشل

3. ⇒ واشك

يُوشِكُ أَنْ يَكُونَ It will soon be: (Ṣ, Mṣb, Ḳ, TA:) or it is near to being. (Mṣb, TA.)

وَشْكَانٌ

وَشْكَانٌ an anomalous inf. n., or perhaps a simple subst.: see 1 in art. شنأ.

وَشِيكًا

وَشِيكًا Quickly, or speedily. (IB, TA.) See an ex. in a verse cited voce تَارَةٌ.
