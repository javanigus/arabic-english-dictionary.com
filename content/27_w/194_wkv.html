وكت وكث وكح

10. ⇒ استوكث

استوكث He ate of what is called وكاث: (Ḳ:) he hastened, and ate somewhat of that which is called وكاث, thereby to attain the period of the morning-meal called الغَدَآء. (L.)

وِكَاثٌ

وِكَاثٌ and وُكَاثٌ Food, aliment, or nutriment, (غِذَآءٌ: so in some copies of the Ḳ, and in the TA: in the CK, غَدَآءٌ, or a morning meal:) that is prepared, or taken, in haste, (يُسْتَعْجَلُ بِهِ,) [before the morning-meal called غَدَآء: see 10]. (Ḳ.)
