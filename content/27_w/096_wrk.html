ورق ورك ورل

وَرِكٌ

وَرِكٌ What is above the thigh; [the haunch; or hip; and often signifying only the hip-bone; and the hip as meaning the joint of the thigh?] (Ṣ, Ḳ, &c.)

مَوْرِكٌ

مَوْرِكٌ of a camel's saddle: see 8 in art. عقل.
