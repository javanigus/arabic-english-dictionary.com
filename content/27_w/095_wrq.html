ورف ورق ورك

وَرِقٌ

وَرِقٌ Silver, whether coined or not: (AO, TA:) or coined dirhems; (Ṣ, Ḳ;) coined silver. (Mgh.) See عَيْنٌ.

أَوْرَاق / وَرَقٌ

أَوْرَاق, pl. of وَرَقٌ, meaning أَحْدَاث: see قَعْبٌ.

وُرْقَةٌ

وُرْقَةٌ Ash-colour. (Mṣb.) See سُمْرَةٌ.

وَرِقَةٌ / وَرِيقَةٌ

وَرِقَةٌ and وَرِيقَةٌ: see وَارِقٌ.

وَارِقٌ

شَجَرٌ وَارِقٌ Trees having leaves: (Mṣb:) [or leafy trees; trees having many leaves; for] شَجَرَةٌ وَارِقَةٌ (TA) andوَرِقَةٌ↓ andوَرِيقَةٌ↓ (Ṣ, Ḳ, TA) signify a tree having many leaves. (Ṣ, Ḳ, TA.) And شَجَرَةٌ وَارِقَةُ الظِّلَالِ [A tree having leafy coverings or shades]. (Ḳ in art. غيل.)

أَوْرَقُ

أَوْرَقُ, applied to a camel, White inclining to black; i. e. of a dusky white hue: or rather, simply, duskish; or dusky; (Ṣ, Ḳ;) or of a colour like that of ashes. (T, Mgh, Mṣb.) See أَحْمَرُ and خُطَبَانِىٌّ.

Root: ورق - Entry: أَوْرَقُ ― Signification: A2

أَوْرَقُ Ashes. (Ḳ.) See an ex. a verse cited voce عُنَّةٌ, last sentence.
