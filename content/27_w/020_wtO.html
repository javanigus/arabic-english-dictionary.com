وت وتأ وتب

1. ⇒ وتأ

وَتَأَ, aor. يَتَأُ, (Ḳ,) inf. n. وَتْءٌ, (TA,) He was heavy and slow in his gait, by reason of age, or by nature. (Ḳ.) Not mentioned by J. because esteemed by him not chaste. (TA.)

تَيْتَأ

تَيْتَأ: see art. تأ.
