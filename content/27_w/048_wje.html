وجه وجى وح

1. ⇒ وجى

وَجِىَ andتَوَجَّى↓ [said of a man, or of a horse or the like, or of a camel] His foot, or hoof, or فِرْسِن, was, or became, attenuated, and chafed, or abraded: (TA:) or he (a horse) experienced a pain in his hoof. (Ṣ.)

5. ⇒ توجّى

تَوَجَّىَ see 1.
