وأم وأى وب

1. ⇒ وأى

وَأَى as syn. with وَعَدَ; imperative إِه. with the ه of silence added; fem. corroborated form of the imperative إِنَّ; of which last, see a curious ex. in the end of article حَرْفٌ الأَلِفِ, in the Mughnee.
