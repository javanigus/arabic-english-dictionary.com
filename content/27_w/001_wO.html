و وأ وأب

R. Q. 1. ⇒ وأوأ

وَأْوَأَ, inf. n. وَأْوَأَهٌ, He (a dog) barked. (A.)

Root: وأ - Entry: R. Q. 1. ― Signification: A2

Also inf. n. وَأْوَآءٌ ([respecting the form of which see ظَأْظَأَ] in the CK and a MṢ. copy, وَأْوَأٌ) He (a jackal, اِبْنُ آوَى,) cried, or barked. (Ḳ.)

Root: وأ - Entry: R. Q. 1. ― Signification: A3

[See also وَعْوَعَ.]
