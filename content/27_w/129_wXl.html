وشك وشل وشم

وَشَلٌ

وَشَلٌ, applied to water: see an ex. voce سَجَعَ; and another in a verse cited voce دَقَلٌ; and another voce رَاشِحٌ.

وَشُولٌ

وَشُولٌA she-camel whose milk lasts throughout the year. (IAạr., in TA, art. شكر.)
