ولج ولح ولد

وَلِيحَةٌ

وَلِيحَةٌ A [sack of the kind called] غِرَارَة: (Ṣ, L, Ḳ:) or such as is called a جُوَالِق: or a large and wide جوالق: (L:) and a date-basket of palm-leaves; syn. جُلَّةٌ: (Ṣ, L, Ḳ:) and a sack or the like forming one half of a beast's load, in which are carried perfumes and clothes (بَزّ) and the like: (L:) n. un. of وَلِيجٌ, pl. وَلَائِحُ. (Ṣ, L, Ḳ.)
