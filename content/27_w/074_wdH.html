ودج ودح ودع

4. ⇒ اودح

اودح He confessed; syn. أَقَرَّ: (L, Ḳ:) or he confessed a falsehood, or what was false: (ISk, T, Ḳ:) or he confessed himself submissive to him who would lead, guide, or govern, him: (AZ, T, Ḳ:) he was submissive, or prompt in obedience, and humble. (Ṣ, Ḳ.)

Root: ودح - Entry: 4. ― Signification: A2

He (a ram) held back, and would not mount the female. (Ṣ, Ḳ.)

Root: ودح - Entry: 4. ― Signification: A3

اودحت الإِبِلُ The camels became fat and in good condition. (Ṣ, Ḳ.)
