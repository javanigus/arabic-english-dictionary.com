وأى وب وبأ

1. ⇒ وبّ

وَبَّ (originally أَبَّ, the أ being changed into و, Az,) inf. n. وَبٌّ; andوَبَوبَ↓, inf. n. وَبْوَبَةٌ; He prepared to charge, or make an assault, in battle. (Ḳ.) See art. أب.

R. Q. 1. ⇒ وبوب

وَبْوَبَ: see 1.
