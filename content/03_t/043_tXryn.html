تسع تشرين تع

تِشْرِينُ

تِشْرِينُ [in Chaldee תִשְרִי] A Greek name of each of two months, (Ḳ,) of the months of Autumn, called تِشْرِينُ الأَوَّلُ and تِشْرِينُ الثَّانِى, [and both together تِشْرِينَانِ, the two Syrian months corresponding, respectively, to October and November O. Ṣ.,] before the two months whereof each is called كَانُونُ. (TA.)
