همأ همت همج

1. ⇒ همت

همَتَ, [aor. ـُ {يَهْمُتُ},] It (ثَرِيد) became hidden in the grease; (Ḳ;) became overspread by the grease. (TA.)

4. ⇒ اهمت

اهمت الكَلَامَ, and الضَّحِكَ, He made speech, and laughter, low; he spoke, and laughed, low. (Ḳ.) It is said to be from الهَمْسُ; the س being changed into ت. (MF.)
