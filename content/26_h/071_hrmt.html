هرم هرمت هرن

هَرَامِيتُ

هَرَامِيتُ Wells: (Ḳ;) a pl. that has no sing.; or its sing. is هِرْمِيتٌ or هُرْمُوتٌ; or perhaps the ت is an augmentative letter: (MF:) or, accord. to the L, it is a name of a certain group of wells in the tract of Ed-Dahma, said to have been dug by Lukmán the son of 'Ád: or, accord. to Aṣ, certain wells on the left of Dareeyeh: if so, F has erred in prefixing to it the art. ال. (TA.)
