هجس هجع هجل

5. ⇒ تهجّع

تَهَجَّعَ He slept. (1001 Nights, ii. 321.)

هَجْعَةٌ

هَجْعَةٌ † The setting of a star. (Ṣgh in TA, art. شرط.) See شَرَطٌ.

Root: هجع - Entry: هَجْعَةٌ ― Signification: A2

A light sleep in the first part of the night. (Ṣ.)
