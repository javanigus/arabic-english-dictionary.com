هش هشم هصب

1. ⇒ هشم

هَشَمَهُ He crushed it.

2. ⇒ هشّم

هَشَّمَهُ, inf. n. تَهْشِيمٌ, He broke it [much, or so crushed it]; (TA;) namely, a dry thing, and anything hollow, such as the head, and the like. (TA in art. شدخ.)

5. ⇒ تهشّم

تَهَشَّمَ It (a plant, or herbage,) became هَشِيم, i. e. dry, and broken in pieces.

هَشِيمٌ

هَشِيمٌ A plant that is dry, and breaks, or is broken, in pieces. (Ṣ, Mṣb, Ḳ.)

هَاشِمَةٌ

هَاشِمَةٌ A wound in the head which breaks the bone: see شَجَّةٌ.

الهَاشِمِىُّ

الكُرُّ الهَاشِمِىُّ: see كُرٌّ.
