هزأ هزب هزبر

هَوْزَبٌ

هَوْزَبٌ A camel strong in running; syn. قَوِىُّ الجَرْىِ: (Ḳ, and so in a copy of the Ṣ:) or a strong and bold camel; syn. قَوِىٌّ جَرِئٌ: (so in the Ṣ, in several copies): a strong camel: (El-Jarmee:) a camel advanced in age, and bold. (Aṣ.)

Root: هزب - Entry: هَوْزَبٌ ― Signification: A2

هَوْزَبٌ A vulture, (Ḳ,) advanced in age. (TA.)

هَيْزَبٌ

هَيْزَبٌ Sharp; fierce; syn. حَدِيدٌ.

Root: هزب - Entry: هَيْزَبٌ ― Signification: A2

ليْثٌ هَيْزَبُ A sharp, or fierce, lion: (Ḳ, accord. to the TA:) or a strong lion. (CK.)

هَازِبَى

هَازِبَى and هَازِبَاءُ A kind of fish. (Ḳ.)
