هلك هلم هم

هَلُمَّ

هَلُمَّ i. q. نَعَالَ Come. (Ṣ, Ḳ, &c.)

Root: هلم - Entry: هَلُمَّ ― Signification: A2

It is intrans.; as in هَلُمَّ إِلَيْنَا Come to us. And tran also; as in هَلُمَّ شُهَدَآءَ كُمْ Cause your witnesses to come; bring your witnesses. (Mṣb.)

Root: هلم - Entry: هَلُمَّ ― Signification: A3

هَلُمَّ جَرًّا At thine ease: see 1 in art. جر.
