هجأ هجب هجد

1. ⇒ هجب

هَجَبَ, aor. ـُ {يَهْجُبُ}, inf. n. هَجْبٌ, He drove, or urged along. (Ḳ.)

Root: هجب - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A2

Also, He beat with a stick, or staff. (Ḳ.)

Root: هجب - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A3

Also, هَجَبَ, inf. n. as above, He was quick, or swift, (Ḳ,) in his pace, &c. (TA.)

Root: هجب - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A4

This art. is omitted by most of the lexicographers, because not regarded by them as of established authority. (TA.)
