هقر هقع هك

الهَقْعَةُ

الهَقْعَةُ Three small stars [λ, φ 1, and φ 2, of Orion,] forming the points of a triangle, in the head of الجُوْزاَءُ The 5th Mansion of the Moon. (El-Ḳazweenee.) [This is accord. to those who make نَوْءٌ to signify the “auroral setting:” accord. to those who make it to signify the “auroral rising,” these stars compose الهَنْعَةُ, q. v.; and الهَفْعَةُ seems to consist of ο 1 and ο 2 of Orion.]
