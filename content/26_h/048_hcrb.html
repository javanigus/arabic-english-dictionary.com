Q. 1. ⇒ هذرب

هَذْرَبَ, inf. n. هَذْرَبَةٌ, He talked much and quickly: (Ḳ:) a dial form of هَذْرَمَ, or mispronounced for the latter word. (TA.) See also هَزْرب.

هُذَيرِبًى

هُذَيرِبًى (accord to the TA, هَذَيْرَبًى, and so in Golius's Lex.,) Custom; habit Ex. هٰذِهِ هذيرباهُ This is his custom, or habit. (Ḳ.)

هُذْرُبَانٌ

هُذْرُبَانٌ Light, or active, (and quick. TA,) in his speech and in his service. (Ḳ.) See also هِذْرِيانٌ, in art. هذر.
