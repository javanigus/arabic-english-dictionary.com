هيم هين هيه

1. ⇒ هين ⇒ هان

هَانَ لَهُ, aor. يَهِينُ, He treated him with gentleness, or blandishment; syn. دَارَاهُ. (Aboo-Is-ḥáḳ, in TA, art. عن; but only the imp., هِنْ لَهُ, is there mentioned, said to be with kesr.) See عَزَّ, near the end, p. 2031, and the distinction there made between هِنْ and هُنْ.
