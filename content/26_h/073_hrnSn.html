هرن هرنصن هرى

هرنصانة

هرنصانة The هرنصانة, (Ḳ,) i. e. هِرْنِصَانَة, (TA,) or هِرِنْصَانَة, (so in the CK, and in my MṢ. copy of the Ḳ,) with kesr, (Ḳ,) and the ر quiescent, and with kesr to the ن, (TA,) is A certain worm, (Ḳ,) accord. to IAạr; said by others to be (TA) what is called the سُرْفَة [which is very variously described]. (Ḳ, TA.) See دُخْلُلٌ.
