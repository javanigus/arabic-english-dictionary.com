هدف هدل هدم

1. ⇒ هدل

هَدَلَ He uttered a cry: see هَدَرَ, in two places.

5. ⇒ تهدّل

تَهَدَّلَ It hung down; [it dangled;] said of a branch of a tree, (Ṣ, TA,) and of fruit; it hung loosely; said of the former. (TA.)

أَهْدَلُ

مِشْفَرٌ أَهْدَلُ [A camel's lip] flaccid, or pendulous. (Ḳ, TA.)
