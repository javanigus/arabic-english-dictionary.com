همذ همر همرج

1. ⇒ همر

هَمَرَهُ, (Ṣ, A, Ḳ,) aor. ـُ {يَهْمُرُ} (Ṣ, Ḳ) and ـِ {يَهْمِرُ}, (Ḳ,) inf. n. هَمْرٌ, (Ṣ,) He, or it, poured it; out or forth; (Ṣ, A, Ḳ;) namely, water, (Ṣ, TA,) and tears, and rain, and the like. (TA.)

Root: همر - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A2

هَمَرَ مَا فِى الضَّرْعِ He drew forth all the milk that was in the udder. (Ṣ, Ḳ.)

Root: همر - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A3

هَمَرَ لَهُ مِنْ مَالِهِ † He gave to him of his property. (Ṣ, Ḳ.)

Root: همر - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A4

هَمَرَ الكَلَامَ, (Ḳ,) or فى كَلَامِهِ, (A,) aor. ـُ {يَهْمُرُ}, inf. n. هَمْرٌ: (TA;) andإِنْهَمَرَ↓ بِالكَلَامِ; (Ṣ;) ‡ He talked much. (Ḳ.)

Root: همر - Entry: 1. ＝ Dissociation: B

هَمَرَ, intrans.: see 7. in two places.

7. ⇒ انهمر

انهمر It poured; poured out or forth; (Ḳ;) said of rain, and of tears; (TA;) as alsoهَمَرَ↓, (Ḳ,) aor. ـِ {يَهْمِرُ}, inf. n. هُمُورٌ; (TA;) [and so, app., اهتمر↓, q. v.:] it flowed; said of water, (Ṣ, Ḳ,) of rain, and of tears; like انهمل: (TA:) and in like manner,هَمَرَتْ↓ عَيْنُهُ بِالدَّمْعِ His eye flowed with tears; as also هَمَلَتْ. (A.)

Root: همر - Entry: 7. ― Signification: A2

انهمر بالكلام: see 1.

8. ⇒ اهتمر

اهتمر: see 7.

Root: همر - Entry: 8. ― Signification: A2

‡ He (a horse) ran (Ṣ, Ḳ, TA) like a torrent. (TA.)

هَمِرٌ

هَمِرٌ Much sand; as alsoيَهْمُورٌ↓. (Ḳ.)

هَمْرَةٌ

هَمْرَةٌ A fall of rain. (Ḳ.)

Root: همر - Entry: هَمْرَةٌ ― Signification: A2

‡ Angry speech. (Ṣgh, L, Ḳ.)

هَمَرَى

هَمَرَى: see هَمَّارٌ.

هَمَّارٌ

هَمَّارٌ A cloud pouring forth much rain; as alsoهَامِرٌ↓. (Ḳ.)

Root: همر - Entry: هَمَّارٌ ― Signification: A2

Applied to a man, (Ṣ,) ‡ Loquacious, garrulous; babbling; a great talker; a babbler; or nonsensical, irrational, foolish, or delirious, in his talk; as alsoمِهْمَرٌ↓ andمِهْمَارٌ↓ (Ṣ, Ḳ) andيَهْمُورٌ↓. (Ṣgh, Ḳ.) Andخَطِيبٌ↓ مِهْمَرٌ ‡ An orator copious in speech. (A, TA) Andهَمَرَى↓, applied to a woman, ‡ Clamorous; (Ḳ, TA:) abounding in talk or speech, like a pouring torrent. (TA.)

هَامِرٌ

هَامِرٌ Pouring rain, and tears; as alsoمُنْهَمِرٌ↓. (TA.) See also هَمَّارٌ.

مِهْمَرٌ: see هَمَّارٌ, in two places.

مِهْمَارٌ: see هَمَّارٌ.

مُنْهَمِرٌ

مُنْهَمِرٌ: see هَامِرٌ.

يَهْمُورٌ

يَهْمُورٌ: see هَمِرٌ:

Root: همر - Entry: يَهْمُورٌ ＝ Dissociation: B
