Quasi شدقم

شَدْقَمٌ

شَدْقَمٌ Wide in the شِدْق [or side of the mouth]; (Ṣ, Ḳ;) applied to a man; as alsoشَدْقَمِىٌّ↓; (TA;) andشُدَاقِمٌ↓: (Ḳ, TA:) [mentioned under this head in the Ṣ and Ḳ, but] the م is augmentative. (Az, Ṣ.) It has been erroneously said that it is with the pointed ذ. (MF.)

Root: شدقم - Entry: شَدْقَمٌ.1 ― Signification: A2

See also أَشْدَقُ.

Root: شدقم - Entry: شَدْقَمٌ.1 ― Signification: A3

Also The lion; (Ḳ;) and soشُدَاقِمٌ↓. (IB, TA.)

Root: شدقم - Entry: شَدْقَمٌ.1 ― Signification: A4

And A certain stallion-camel belonging to En-Noamán Ibn-El-Mundhir: whence شَدْقِمِيَّاتٌ↓ Certain camels, so called in relation to that stallion. (Ṣ, Ḳ.)

شَدْقَمِىٌّ / شَدْقَمِيَّاتٌ

شَدْقَمِىٌّ and شَدْقَمِيَّاتٌ: see the preceding paragraph.

شُدَاقِمٌ

شُدَاقِمٌ: see the first paragraph, in two places.
