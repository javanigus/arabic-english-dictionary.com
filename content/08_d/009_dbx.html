دبح دبخ دبر

2. ⇒ دبّخ

دبّخ, inf. n. تَدْبِيخٌ, He (a man, Ṣ) made his back round like a dome (قَبَّبَهُ) and lowered his head; (Ṣ, Ḳ;) as also دبّح [q. v.]; with ح and خ, on the authority of AA and IAạr. (Ṣ.)

دُبَّاخٌ

دُبَّاخٌ A certain game [app. that which is described in the first paragraph of art. دبح.] (Ḳ.)
