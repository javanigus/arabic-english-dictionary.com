دلو دلى دم

1. ⇒ دلى

دَلِىَ, (IAạr, T, Ḳ,) aor. ـَ {يَدْلَىُ}, inf. n. دَلًا, (TḲ,) He was, or became, confounded, or perplexed, and unable to see his right course. (IAạr, T, Ḳ.)

3. ⇒ دالى

mentioned in this art. in the Ḳ: see art. دلو.

5. ⇒ تدلّى

mentioned in this art. in the Ḳ: see art. دلو.
