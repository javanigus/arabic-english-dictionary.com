يهت يهمت يوح

اليَهْمُوتُ

اليَهْمُوتُ A name of the fish upon which is the earth: erroneously written with ب [البهموت]: so says Esh-Shiháb in the 'Imáyeh. (MF, TA.) [See my translation of the 1001 Nights, note to the Introduction.]
