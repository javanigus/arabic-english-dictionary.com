يثم يد يربع

يَدٌ

يَدٌ The arm, from the shoulder-joint to the extremities of the fingers. (Mṣb.)

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A2

[The foreleg of a horse, &c.]

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A3

يَدٌ بَيْضَآءُ: see أَبْيَضُ, in two places, near the end of the paragraph.

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A4

يَدٌ A sleeve: see R. Q. 1 in art. ذب.

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A5

يَدُ القَوْسِ: see رِجْلٌ, in two places.

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A6

طِوَالُ الأَيْدِ for الأَيْدِى: see ثَمَانِيَةٌ.

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A7

أَعْطَاهُ عَنْ يَدٍ, as occurring in the Ḳur, ix., 29, He gave it in acknowledgement of the superiority of the receiver; that the power (يَدْ) of the latter was superior to that of the giver: or, because of favour received; or, from subjection and abasement: (M:) or, from compulsion: (AʼObeyd, T:) or, obediently: or, walking with it; not riding, nor sending it: or, in ready money. (TA.)

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A8

أَسْلَمَ عَلَى يَدَىْ فُلَانٍ He became a Muslim by the advice and persuasion of such a one. (Marg. note in a copy of the Jámiʼ eṣ-Ṣagheer, on a trad. commencing مَنْ أَسْلَمَ.)

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A9

أَخَذتُّ عِنْدَهُ يَدًا: see art. اخذ.

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A10

لِفُلَانٍ عِنْدِى يَدٌ I owe such a one a benefit.

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A11

عَلَى يَدِهِ By his agency, or means. See the corresponding expression in Hebrew, in Ps. lxiii. 11, Jer. xviii. 21, and Ezek. xxxv. 5, in the phrase “to pour out (the blood of) a person by means of the sword.”

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A12

وَلَا تُلْقُوا بِأَيْدِيكُمْ إِلَى التَّهْلُكَةِ (Ḳur, ii. 191): see ب (used redundantly).

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A13

لا آتِيهِ يَدَ الدَّهْرِ, and يَدَ المُسْنَدِ I will not come to him, or do it, ever; I will never do it. (IAạr, in I., voce مُسْنَدٌ.) See أَبَدٌ and جَدًا.

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A14

عَلَى يَدَىِ الخَيْرِ وَاليُمْنِ: see خَيْرٌ.

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A15

بِعْتُهُ يَدًا بِيَدٍ: see نَاجِزٌ.

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A16

You say, also, بَايَعْتُهُ يَدًا بِيَدٍ, the two nouns being only thus used, as a denotative of state, I contracted a sale with him for ready money; i. q. بِالتَّعْجِيلِ وَالنَّقْدِ. (Mgh.)

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A17

لَكَ أَنْ يَكُونَ كَذَا [or يَدَىَّ?] يَدِىَّ is like عَلَىَّ لك ان يكون كذا. (Aboo-Sahl El-Harawee, TA in art. حبق.)

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A18

أُتِىَ عَلَى يَدِ فُلَانٍ: see أَتَى.

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A19

أَوَّلَ ذِى يَدَيْنِ and ذَاتِ يَدَيْنِ: see art. ذو.

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A20

تَحْتَ يَدِهِ Under his authority.

Root: يد - Entry: يَدٌ ― Signification: A21

يَدٌ † Generosity. (A, voce شَبْرٌ)

مَيْدِىٌّ

مَيْدِىٌّ A gazelle whose fore-leg is caught in a snare: see مَرْجُولٌ.
