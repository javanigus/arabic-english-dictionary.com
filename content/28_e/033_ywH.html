يهمت يوح يوم

يُوحُ

يُوحُ, (so accord. to AḤát, Mbr, AAF, Kr, Abu-l-'Ala El-Ma'arree, A,) [of the fem. gender, (see بُوحُ,)] indecl., and without the art. ال (ISd,) accord. to IAmb بُوحُ, but this is a mistake originating from a corrupt transcription of the word, (IKh, IB, &c.,) and يُوحِ, also indecl. like بَرَاحِ, (TA) and يُوحَى, (IAth, Ḳ,) names of The sun. (Ṣ, art. بوح, Ḳ, &c.) [See بُوحُ.]
