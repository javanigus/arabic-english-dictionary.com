سن سنبق سنبك

سُنْبُوقٌ

سُنْبُوقٌ A small زَوْرَق [or skiff], (Ṣgh, Ḳ, TA,) made on the coasts of the sea: a word of the dial. of the people of all the coasts of the Sea of El-Yemen: (Ṣgh, TA:) whether the ن be radical requires consideration: Ṣgh says, in the Tekmileh, that the word is of the measure فُنْعُولٌ, from السَّبْقُ. (TA.)
