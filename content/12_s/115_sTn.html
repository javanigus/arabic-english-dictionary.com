سطل سطن سطو

[2. {سطّن}]

[سطّن a verb of which only the pass. part. n. (q. v. infrà) is mentioned: if used, app. signifying He supported upon أَسَاطِين, i. e. columns:]

Root: سطن - Entry: 2. ― Signification: A2

[and hence, upon long legs:]

Root: سطن - Entry: 2. ― Signification: A3

[and he made firm.]

سَاطِنٌ

سَاطِنٌ i. q. خَبِيثٌ [Bad, corrupt,, &c.; like شَاطِنٌ]. (M, L, Ḳ.)

أَسْطَانٌ

أَسْطَانٌ [a pl. of which the sing. is not mentioned] Vessels of صُفْر [or brass]. (L, Ḳ.)

أُسْطُوَانٌ

أُسْطُوَانٌ, an arabicized word, (Az, L,) [app. from the Pers. أُسْتُونْ, like the word next following,] A man long in the legs and back. (M, L.) And A camel long in the neck, (M, L, Ḳ,) or (Ḳ) high. (Ṣ, M, L, Ḳ.)

Root: سطن - Entry: أُسْطُوَانٌ ― Signification: A2

What is termed أُسْتُوَانُ البَيْتِ is well known [app. meaning The pole of the tent: (see what next follows:) and this seems to be the primary signification]. (M, L.)

أُسْطُوَانَةٌ

أُسْطُوَانَةٌ a word of well-known meaning, (Ṣ, L,) arabicized from [the Pers.] أُسْتُوِنْ [which signifies the same], (Ḳ,) i. q. سَارِيَةٌ [meaning A column, of stone or of baked bricks]: (Ṣ, M, L, Mṣb, Ḳ:) pl. أَسَاطِينُ (Ṣ, M,* L, Mṣb, Ḳ *) and أُسْطُوَانَاتٌ: (Mṣb:) it is of the measure أُفْعُوَالَةٌ, (Ṣ, L, Mṣb, Ḳ,) because its pl. is أَسَاطَينُ, the ن being radical, (L, Mṣb,) accord. to Kh (Mṣb) and Fr, who says that it is the only instance of this measure; (L;) or of the measure فُعْلُوَانَةٌ, (Ṣ, L, Ḳ,) accord. to Akh, but, (J says, L,) if so, the و is augmentative and has next to it two augmentative letters, and this is scarcely ever, or never, the case; (Ṣ, L;) IB, however, says that this is the true measure, as is shown by its pl., أَسَاطِينُ, and its dim., أُسَيْطِينَةٌ↓: (L:) accord. to some, it is of the measure أُفْعُلَانَةٌ, (Ṣ, L, Mṣb,) but, if it were so, it would not have for its pl. أَسَاطِينُ, as there is no instance of the measure أَفَاعِينُ. (Ṣ, L.)

Root: سطن - Entry: أُسْطُوَانَةٌ ― Signification: A2

[Also A portico. Hence, أَهْلُ الأُسْطُوَانَةِ The Stoics.]

Root: سطن - Entry: أُسْطُوَانَةٌ ― Signification: A3

[And A cylinder.]

Root: سطن - Entry: أُسْطُوَانَةٌ ― Signification: A4

And The legs of a beast: (Ḳ:) [or rather the pl.] أَسَاطِينُ has this meaning. (L.)

Root: سطن - Entry: أُسْطُوَانَةٌ ― Signification: A5

And The penis. (Ḳ.)

أُسَيْطِينَةٌ

أُسَيْطِينَةٌ dim. of أُسْطُوَانَةٌ, q. v. (IB, L.)

مُسَطَّنٌ

مُسَطَّنٌ [app. Supported upon أَسَاطِين, i. e. columns.]

Root: سطن - Entry: مُسَطَّنٌ ― Signification: A2

[And hence,] A man, and a beast, having long legs. (L.)

Root: سطن - Entry: مُسَطَّنٌ ― Signification: A3

And أَسَاطِينُ مُسَطَّنَةٌ (Ṣ, M, L, Ḳ,) [Columns] made firm. (Ḳ.)
