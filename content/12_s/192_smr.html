Root: سمر - Entry: مَسْمُورٌ ＝ Dissociation: B

عَيْشٌ مَسْمُورٌ ‡ A turbid life: (M, O,* Ḳ,* TA:) from سَمَارٌ applied to milk. (M, TA.)
