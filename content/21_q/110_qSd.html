مُقْتَصِدٌ

فُلَانٌ مُقْتَصِدٌ فِى النَّفَقَةِ ‡ Such a one acts in a moderate manner, in a manner between that of prodigality and that of parsimoniousness, in expense. (Ṣ, L.) See 1. And see مُقَصَّدٌ.
