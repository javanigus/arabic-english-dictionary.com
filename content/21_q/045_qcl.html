قذف قذل قذى

الفَذَالُ

الفَذَالُ The whole of the back of the head: (Ṣ, Mṣb, Ḳ:) or the part from the hollow of the back of the neck (نُقْرَة القَفَا) to the ear: (El-Ghooree, Mgh:) [see القَمَحْدُوَةُ in art. قحد:] and, in a horse, the place where the عِذَار is tied, behind the forelock. (Ṣ, Mṣb, Ḳ.)
