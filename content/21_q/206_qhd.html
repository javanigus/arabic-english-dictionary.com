قهب قهد قهر

قَهْدٌ

قَهْدٌ Clear in colour. (L, Ḳ.)

Root: قهد - Entry: قَهْدٌ ― Signification: A2

White; as also قَهْبٌ: (AʼObeyd, L:) by some specially applied to the young of antelopes and cows: (L:) or of a dingy, or dusky, white; as also قَهْبٌ. (Ṣ, L.)
