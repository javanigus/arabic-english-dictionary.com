قَلَمٌ

قَلَمٌ andقَلَمَانِ↓ andقَلَمَانٌ↓: see جَلَمٌ.

Root: قلم - Entry: قَلَمٌ ― Signification: A2

A writing-reed prepared for writing; a reed-pen.
