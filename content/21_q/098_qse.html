قسم قسى قش

3. ⇒ قاسى

قَاسَاهُ He endured it; struggled, or contended, with, or against, it; struggled, or contended, with, or against, its difficulty, or severity; he endured, or he struggled, or contended, with, or against, the difficulty, or trouble, or inconvenience, that he experienced from it or him; [and so عَانَاهُ; for both of which see Ḥar, p. 564; and for the latter see شَاقَاهُ; and for both see also مُجَانَاةٌ]; syn. كَابَدَهُ, (Ṣ, Ḳ,) and عَالَجَ شِدَّتَهُ: (TA:) he underwent difficulties, troubles, or inconveniences, in doing it. (Mṣb in explanation of the syn. كَابَدَهُ.)
