قوط قوع قوف

قَاعٌ

قَاعٌ An even place; (Ṣ, voce مَقَدٌّ;) plain, or level, land, (Ṣ, Mṣb,) that produces nothing; (IF, Mṣb;) plain, or soft, land, (أَرْضٌ سَهْلَةٌ,) low, and free from mountains. (Ḳ.) See also رَوْضَةٌ. Respecting its pls., see جَارٌ.
