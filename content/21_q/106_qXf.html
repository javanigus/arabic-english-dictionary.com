قشعر قشف قشم

1. ⇒ قشف

قَشِفَ, inf. n. قَشَفَ, He was coarse in his living: this is the original signification: he was neglectful of cleanliness: (Mṣb:) he was neglectful of washing and cleanliness; unfrequent in paying attention thereto; slovenly with respect to his person: (M, Mṣb:) andتَقَشَّفَ↓ signifies the like. (Mṣb.)

Root: قشف - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A2

قَشِفَ, inf. n. قَشَفٌ, He became sunburnt. (M.)

5. ⇒ تقشّف

see 1.
