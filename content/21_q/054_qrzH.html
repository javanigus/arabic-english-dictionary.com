قرد قرزح قرس

قُرْزُحٌ

قُرْزُحٌ, (AA, Ṣ, Ḳ,) and قُرْزُوحٌ, (TA, and so in one copy of the Ṣ.) A certain species of tree: (Ṣ, Ḳ:) n. un. قُرْزُحَةٌ. (TA.)

Root: قرزح - Entry: قُرْزُحٌ ― Signification: A2

Also قُرْزُحَةٌ A certain herb, or leguminous plant. (Kr, Ḳ.)

Root: قرزح - Entry: قُرْزُحٌ ― Signification: A3

And A certain small tree, (AḤn, Ḳ,) curling and contracting, (جَعْدَةٌ,) and having black جَبّ [i. e. grains, or berries, or the like]. (AḤn.)
