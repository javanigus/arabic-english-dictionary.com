قرزح قرس قرش

1. ⇒ قرس

قَرَسَ, aor. ـِ {يَقْرِسُ}, (Ṣ, A, Ḳ,) inf, n. قَرْسٌ, (Ṣ,) It (cold) was, or became, intense, or vehement; (Ṣ, A, Ḳ;) as also قَرِسَ, aor. ـَ {يَقْرَسُ}, (Ṣ, Ḳ,) inf. n. قَرَسٌ. (Ṣ.)

Root: قرس - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A2

It (water) became congealed, or frozen. (Ṣ, Ḳ.)

Root: قرس - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A3

قرس, inf. n. قرس, [so in the TA, without any syll. signs,] He (a man) was, or became, cold. (TA.) [The verb and its inf. n. in this sense are probably the same as in the sense here next following.]

Root: قرس - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A4

قُرِسَ, inf. n. قَرْسٌ; (TA;) or قَرِسَ, [inf. n. قَرَسٌ;] (JK;) He (a man smitten by cold) became unable to work (JK, TA) with his hands, (JK,) or with his hand, by reason of the intenseness of the cold, or, as in the L, by reason of cold in his extremities. (TA.)

Root: قرس - Entry: 1. ＝ Dissociation: B

قَرَسَ المَآءَ: see 4.

Root: قرس - Entry: 1. ＝ Dissociation: C

قَرَسَ قَرِيسًا, (TA,) orقرّسهُ↓, (accord. to a copy of the A,) He made, or prepared, what is termed قريس, (A,* TA,) i. e., broth with flesh-meat. (A.)

2. ⇒ قرّس

see 4, in two places:

Root: قرس - Entry: 2. ＝ Dissociation: B

and see 1, last signification.

4. ⇒ اقرس

اقرس العُوُد The branch, or twig, had its sap congealed in it. In the M, instead of جَمَسَ مَاؤُهُ فِيهِ, we find حَبَسَ فِيهِ مَاؤُهُ [which is probably a mistake of a copyist]. (TA.)

Root: قرس - Entry: 4. ＝ Dissociation: B

اقرسهُ البَرْدُ [The cold] made him cold; as alsoقرّسهُ↓, inf. n. تَقْرِيسٌ: (Ṣ, Ḳ:) [or,] accord. to some, by البرد is here meant sleep: (TA:) or the cold made him unable to work with his hand: (JK:) and اقرس البَرْدُ أَصَابِعَهُ the cold made his fingers rigid, by chilness of the extremities, so that he was unable to work. (A, L.)

Root: قرس - Entry: 4. ― Signification: B2

اقرس المَآءَ فِى الشَّنِّ He cooled the water in the old worn-out skin; (AʼObeyd, TA.) as alsoقرّسهُ↓ فيه; (AʼObeyd, Ṣ, A;) andقَرَسَهُ↓ فيه, inf. n. قَرْسٌ. (AʼObeyd, TA.)

قَرْسٌ

قَرْسٌ Intense, or vehement, cold; (Ṣ, A, Ḳ;) as alsoقَارِسٌ↓ andقَرِيسٌ↓. (Ḳ.) You say, لَيْلَةٌ ذَاتُ فَرْسٍ A night of [intense] cold. (Ṣ.)

Root: قرس - Entry: قَرْسٌ ― Signification: A2

The densest and coldest hoar-frost or rime: (Lth, JK, Ḳ:) or the coldest and most copious hoarfrost or rime; as alsoقَرَسٌ↓. (M, TA.)

Root: قرس - Entry: قَرْسٌ ― Signification: A3

See also قَارِسٌ.

قَرَسٌ

قَرَسٌ: see قَرْسٌ:

Root: قرس - Entry: قَرَسٌ ― Signification: A2

and قَارِسٌ.

قَرِيسٌ

قَرِيسٌ: see قَارِسٌ, in three places:

Root: قرس - Entry: قَرِيسٌ ― Signification: A2

and قَرْسٌ.

Root: قرس - Entry: قَرِيسٌ ＝ Dissociation: B

Broth with flesh-meat. (A.)

Root: قرس - Entry: قَرِيسٌ ＝ Dissociation: C

سَمَكٌ قَرِيسٌ Fish that is cooked, and for which a sauce (صِبَاغ) is then made, in which it is left until it becomes concreted: (Ṣ:) or cooked fish in which a sauce is made, wherein it is left until it becomes concreted, (Ḳ,* TA,) but neither congealed nor fluid; [being converted into a gelatinous substance;] as also قَرِيصٌ: the former is of the dial. of Keys. (TA.)

قَارِسٌ

قَارِسٌ Intense, or vehement, cold; as alsoقَرِيسٌ↓: you should not say قَارِصٌ. (Ṣ.)

Root: قرس - Entry: قَارِسٌ ― Signification: A2

See also قَرْسٌ.

Root: قرس - Entry: قَارِسٌ ― Signification: A3

In a state of congelation, or freezing; as alsoقَرِيسٌ↓; (Ṣ;) andقَرَسٌ↓: (IAạr, ISk, Ṣ, Ḳ:) the first and second applied to water: (Ṣ:) the last, to anything; (IAạr;) but this last was unknown to Abu-l-Gheyth. (Ṣ.)

Root: قرس - Entry: قَارِسٌ ― Signification: A4

Cold; chill; as alsoقَرِيسٌ↓, (TA,) andقَرْسٌ↓. (Ḳ.) You say يَوْمٌ قَارِسٌ [A cold day]. (A, TA.) And لَيْلَةٌ قَارِسَةٌ [A cold night]. (TA.)
