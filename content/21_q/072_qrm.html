مِقْرَمَةٌ A coverlet of a bed; (Mgh, in arts. قرم and حبس;) also called مِحْبَسٌ: (Id, in art. حبس:) or a thin curtain, accord. to some, figured; as alsoمِقْرَمٌ↓ andقِرَامٌ↓: (Mṣb:) or this last, a figured curtain. (Mṣb.)
