قمهد قن قنأ

قُنَّةٌ

قُنَّةٌ An isolated mountain. (Ḳ, voce جَبَلٌ.) See a verse cited in art. عز.

قِنَّةٌ

قِنَّةٌ Galbanum: so in the present day: see سَكْبِينَجٌ.

قُنَنَةٌ

رَجُلٌ أُنَنَةٌ قُنَنَةٌ: see art. ان. The last word may perhaps be a mistranscription for فُنَنَةٌ (from فَنُّ): but this I have not found in art. فن.

قِنِّيَّةٌ

قِنِّيَّةٌ The state, or condition, of slavery.
