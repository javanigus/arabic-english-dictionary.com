قنفد قنفذ / or / accord. to some / قفذ قنقل

قُنْفُذٌ / قُنْفُذَةٌ / قُنْفَذٌ

قُنْفُذٌ and قُنْفَذٌ [The male hedge-hog;] (Ṣ, L, Mṣb, Ḳ;) i. q. شَيْهَمٌ: (M, L, Ḳ:) or the male and female: (Mṣb:) or the fem. is with ة {قُنْفُذَةٌ}, (Ṣ, L, Mṣb, Ḳ,) sometimes; and the male is called شَيْهَمٌ and دُلْدُلٌ: (Mṣb:) pl. قَنَافِذُ. (Ṣ.) Some hold that the ن is an augmentative letter: others, that it is a radical. (TA.)

Supplement:

قُنْفُذٌ / قُنْفُذَةٌ

مَا أَبْيَضُ شَطْرًا أَسْوَدُ ظَهْرًا يَمْشِى قِمْطَرًا وَيَبُولُ قَطْرًا What is white in one half, black in the back, that walks contractedly, and makes water in drops? An enigma: to which the answer is, The قُنْفُذ [q. v.]. (L.)

Root: قنفذ - Entry: قُنْفُذٌ.1 ― Signification: A2

قُنْفُذُ لَيْلٍ One who sleeps not [during the night]; because the قنفذ sleeps not: (L:) and a calumniator; a slanderer; (L, Ḳ;) as also أَنْقَدُ لَيْلٍ. (L.)

Root: قنفذ - Entry: قُنْفُذٌ.1 ― Signification: A3

Also قُنْفُذٌ and قُنْفَذٌ The rat, or mouse; syn. فَارٌ: (Ḳ:) fem. with ة {قُنْفُذَةٌ}. (TA.)

Root: قنفذ - Entry: قُنْفُذٌ.1 ― Signification: A4

قُنْفُذٌ † The part behind the ear of a camel, whence the sweat exudes. (Ṣ, L, Ḳ.)

Root: قنفذ - Entry: قُنْفُذٌ.1 ― Signification: A5

And, with ة {قُنْفُذَةٌ}, † The part of the head that is below that which is called the قَمَحْدُوَة. (L.)

Root: قنفذ - Entry: قُنْفُذٌ.1 ― Signification: A6

قُنْفُذٌ † A place that produces tangled herbage: (Ṣ, L, Ḳ:) an elevated place abounding with trees: AḤn says, that what is thus termed is in hard ground, between what is termed قُفّ and sand. (L.)

Root: قنفذ - Entry: قُنْفُذٌ.1 ― Signification: A7

قُنْفُذُ رَمْلٍ † Sand collected together, (Aboo-Kheyreh, L, Ḳ,) and elevated, (Ḳ,) or somewhat elevated: (Aboo-Kheyreh, L:) or the abundance of trees of sand: or, as some say, قُنْفَذُ رَمْلٍ, with fet-ḥ, signifies abundance and tallness of trees of sand. (L.)

Root: قنفذ - Entry: قُنْفُذٌ.1 ― Signification: A8

قُنْفُذٌ (L, Ḳ) and with ة {قُنْفُذَةٌ} (L) † A tree in the midst of sand. (L, Ḳ.)

Root: قنفذ - Entry: قُنْفُذٌ.1 ― Signification: A9

قَنَافِذُ † Mountains that are not long: or long and narrow tracts, (أَحْبُل, L, Ḳ, TA: in the CK, أَجْبُل, or mountains,) of sand: (L, Ḳ:) or hills with pointed heads (نَبَكٌ) lying in the way, or road. (Th, L, Ḳ.)
