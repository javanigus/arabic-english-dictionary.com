قرش قرشب قرص

قِرْشَبٌّ

قِرْشَبٌّ Advanced in years: (Aṣ, Ṣ, Ḳ:) applied to a man. (Ṣ.)

Root: قرشب - Entry: قِرْشَبٌّ ― Signification: A2

One who is in a bad state, or condition. (IAạr, Ḳ.)

Root: قرشب - Entry: قِرْشَبٌّ ― Signification: A3

A man of bad disposition. (Kr, Ḳ.)

Root: قرشب - Entry: قِرْشَبٌّ ― Signification: A4

Having a capacious belly. (Ḳ.)

Root: قرشب - Entry: قِرْشَبٌّ ― Signification: A5

A great eater; voracious. (Ḳ.)

Root: قرشب - Entry: قِرْشَبٌّ ― Signification: A6

A man (TA) corpulent, or bulky, and tall. (Ḳ.)

Root: قرشب - Entry: قِرْشَبٌّ ― Signification: A7

القِرْشَبٌّ The lion. (Ḳ.)

Root: قرشب - Entry: قِرْشَبٌّ ― Signification: A8

Pl. (in each of the above senses, TA) قَرَاشِبُ. (Ḳ.) [See also كِرْشَبٌّ.]
