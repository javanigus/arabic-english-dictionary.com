قمحد قمد قمر

1. ⇒ قمد

قَمِدَ, aor. ـَ {يَقْمَدُ}, (TA,) inf. n. قَمَدٌ, (Ḳ,) He, or it, was, or became, tall, or long: or he was, or became, large and long in the neck. (Ḳ, TA.)

Q. Q. 4. ⇒ إِقْمَهَدَّ

إِقْمَهَدَّ [in which the ه is an augmentative letter accord. to J, is said by F to be improperly assigned by J to this art.] see art. قمهد.

قُمُدٌّ

قُمُدٌّ (Lth, Ṣ, L, Ḳ) and قُمُدٌ (Ḳ) or قُمْدٌ (L) and قُمْدُدٌ (Lth, L) and قُمْدُودٌ and قُمَادٌ and قُمَادِىٌّ (Ḳ) and قُمُدَّانٌ and قُمُدَّانِىٌّ (L, Ḳ) Strong: (Lth, Ṣ, Ḳ:) or strong and hard or hardy: (L:) or gross, thick, coarse, or rude, (Ḳ,) and hard, or hardy: (TA:) applied to a man: (L, Ḳ:) fem. قُمُدَّةٌ (Ṣ) and قُمُدَّانَةٌ and قُمُدَّانِيَّةٌ. (L.)

Root: قمد - Entry: قُمُدٌّ ― Signification: A2

إِنَّهُ لَقُمُدٌّ قُمْدُدٌ Verily he is very strong. (Lth, L.)

Root: قمد - Entry: قُمُدٌّ ― Signification: A3

ذَكَرٌ قُمُدٌّ Penis vehementer se erigens, (L, Ḳ,) or القُمُدُّ is a name of the penis. (L.)

Root: قمد - Entry: قُمُدٌّ ― Signification: A4

See also أَقْمَدُ.

أَقْمَدُ

أَقْمَدُ, fem. قَمْدَآءُ, (L, Ḳ,) andقُمُدٌّ↓ and [fem.? see قُمُدُّ above] قُمُدَّةٌ and قُمُدَّانِيَّةٌ, (Ḳ,) Large and long in the neck: or tall, (L, Ḳ,) in a general sense: applied to a human being. (L.)

Root: قمد - Entry: أَقْمَدُ ― Signification: A2

نَحْنُ قُمْدُ الأَقْمَادِ We are thick-necked. (L.)

Root: قمد - Entry: أَقْمَدُ ― Signification: A3

See also قُمُدٌّ.
