قضف قضم قضى

1. ⇒ قضم

قَضِمَ He crunched, nibbled or gnawed? See خَضِمَ: and see also Freytag's Arab. Prov. ii. 245.

قَضَامٌ

قَضَامٌ: see خُضَامَةٌ.

مَقْضَمٌ

مَقْضَمٌ: see خُضَامَةٌ.

قَضِيمٌ

قَضِيمٌ as meaning A skin, &c. has for pl. أَقْضِمَةٌ [a pl. of pauc.] and قُضُمٌ, and قُضْمٌ, accord. to Sb, is a quasi-pl. n., (TA,) and قَضَمٌ is a pl. [or quasi-pl. n.] thereof as meaning a white skin upon which one writes. (Ṣ, Ḳ.) See an ex. of قُضُمٌ voce عَسِيبٌ.
