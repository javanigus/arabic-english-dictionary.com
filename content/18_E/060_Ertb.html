عربن عرتب عرتن

العَرْتَبَةُ

العَرْتَبَةُ a dial. var. of العَرْتَمَةُ; (Ṣ, O;) The nose: or the soft, or pliable, part thereof: or the [depression termed] دَائِرَة beneath the nose, in [or above] the middle of the lip, (Ḳ, TA,) i. e., of the upper lip, next the nose: (TA:) or the extremity of the partition between the nostrils: (Ḳ:) [J says,] I asked an Arab of the desert, of the tribe of Asad, whereupon he put his finger upon the extremity of the partition between his nostrils. (Ṣ.)
