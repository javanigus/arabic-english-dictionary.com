حنذ حنزب حنش

حِنْزَابٌ / حِنْزَابَةٌ

حِنْزَابٌ An ass of middling make (مُقْتَدِرُ الخَلْقِ). (Ḳ.)

Root: حنزب - Entry: حِنْزَابٌ.1 ― Signification: A2

Also Short and strong: or broad: (Ḳ:) or short and broad; applied to a man: (Th, TA:) and thick, coarse, rude, or bulky, (Ḳ, TA,) and short. (TA.) [See حَزَابٍ, in two places.]

Root: حنزب - Entry: حِنْزَابٌ.1 ― Signification: A3

The cock. (Ḳ.)

Root: حنزب - Entry: حِنْزَابٌ.1 ― Signification: A4

Also, andحُنْزُوبٌ↓, A flock of [the kind of birds called] قَطًا: (Ḳ:) or the male of the قطا. (TA.) [See حنزاب in art. حزب.]

Root: حنزب - Entry: حِنْزَابٌ.1 ― Signification: A5

And both these words, The carrot of the land (جَزَرُ البَرِّ [see حنزاب in art. حزب]): (Ḳ:) n. un. of the former with ة {حِنْزَابَةٌ: that of the latter is not known to have been in use. (TA.)

Root: حنزب - Entry: حِنْزَابٌ.1 ― Signification: A6

This is [said to be] the proper place of these two words; [the ن being regarded as a radical letter;] (Ḳ, TA;) not art. حزب. (TA.)

حُنْزُوبٌ

حُنْزُوبٌ: see above: and see also art. حزب.
