1. ⇒ حفل

حَفَلَ, aor. ـِ {يَحْفِلُ}, inf. n. حَفْلٌ and حُفُولٌ and حَفِيلٌ, said of water, and of milk (Ḳ) in the udder (TA) [or breast], It collected; as alsoتحِفّل↓ andاحتفل↓. (Ḳ, TA. [In the CK, احَتَفَلَهُ is erroneously put for احْتَفَلَ.]) And حَفْلٌ signifies The collecting of water, i. e. its becoming collected, in its مَحْفِل, meaning its place of collecting. (TA.)

Root: حفل - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A2

حَفَلَ الدَّمْعُ, (M, Ḳ,) inf. n. حَفْلٌ, (TA,) The tears became copious. (M, Ḳ, TA.) In some copies of the Ḳ, نثر is here erroneously put for كَثُرَ. (TA.)

Root: حفل - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A3

حَفَلَ الوَادِى بِالسَّيْلِ The valley brought the torrent so as that it filled its sides; as alsoاحتفل↓: (Ḳ:) or the latter signifies the valley became filled by the torrent: (Ṣ:) or احتفل الوادى the valley became full, and flowed. (Mṣb.)

Root: حفل - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A4

حَفَلَتِ السَّمَآءُ, (Ṣ, M, Ḳ,) inf. n. حَفْلٌ, (Ṣ,) The sky rained vehemently: (Ḳ:) or the rain fell profusely. (Ṣ,* M, TA.)

Root: حفل - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A5

2. ⇒ حفّل

حفّلُه, (Mgh,* Mṣb, Ḳ,) inf. n. تَحْفِيلٌ; (TA;) andحَفَلَهُ↓, (Ḳ,) inf. n. حَفْلٌ; (TA;) He collected it, or caused it to collect; (Mgh, Mṣb, Ḳ;) namely, water, (Ḳ,) and milk (Mgh, Mṣb, Ḳ) in the udder (Mgh) of a ewe or she-goat, (Mgh, Mṣb,) or of a she-camel, or of a cow, in order to deceive the purchaser, that he might increase the price. (Mgh.) Hence one says, حفّل الشَّاةَ, (Ṣ,* Mṣb, Ḳ,*) or البَقَرَةَ, or النَّاقَةَ, (TA,) inf. n. as above, (Ṣ, Ḳ,) He abstained from milking the ewe or she-goat, (Ṣ, Mṣb, Ḳ,*) or the cow, or the she-camel, (TA,) for some days, in order that the milk might collect in her udder, for sale, (Ṣ, Ḳ,*) or until the milk collected in her udder. (Mṣb.) The Prophet forbade the doing this. (Ṣ.)

He adorned him, or it. (Ḳ,* TA.)
