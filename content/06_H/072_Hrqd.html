حِرْقِدٌ

حِرْقِدٌ: see what follows.

حَرْقَدَةٌ

حَرْقَدَةٌ The عُقَدْة of the حُنْجُور [meaning the head of the windpipe; app. called its عقدة, or “knob,” because its anterior portion forms a protuberance, that which we term “pomum Adami:” see غُصَّةٌ]: (Ṣ, L, Ḳ:) pl. حَرَاقِدُ. (L.)

Root: حرقد - Entry: حَرْقَدَةٌ ― Signification: A2

And The root of the tongue; (IAạr, L;) as alsoحِرْقِدٌ↓. (IAạr, L, Ḳ.)
