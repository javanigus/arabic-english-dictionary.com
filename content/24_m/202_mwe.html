موه موى ميب

مَاهَةٌ

مَاهَةٌ Small-pox: see آهَةٌ in art. اوه.

مَاوِيَّةٌ

مَاوِيَّةٌ A mirror; so called in relation to water, because of its clearness, and because images are seen in it as they are in clear water: the م is a radical letter. (T in art. اوى.)
