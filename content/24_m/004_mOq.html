مأد مأق مأن

مَأْقٌ / مُؤْقٌ / مَأْقٍ

مَأْقٌ and مُؤْقٌ and مَأْقٍ: see مَأوًى.

مَأَقَةٌ

مَأَقَةٌ A sobbing; i. e. an affection like what is termed فُوَاق, as though it were breath heaved from the chest, on an occasion of weeping, and of being choked with weeping. (Ṣ, Ḳ.)
