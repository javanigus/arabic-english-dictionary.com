مرع مرغ مرق

2. ⇒ مرّغ

مَرَّغَ He rolled, or turned over, a beast of carriage, in the dust. (Ḳ.)

Root: مرغ - Entry: 2. ― Signification: A2

مَرَّغَ He smeared, seasoned, imbued, or soaked, a mess of ثَرِيدِ, with grease, or gravy, or dripping; i. q. رَوَّغَ, and دَسَّمَ, and سَغْبَلَ. (TA in art. روغ.)

3. ⇒ مارغ

مَارَغَهُ [He rolled with him upon the ground, or in the dust]; said of a man after his wrestling with another. (TA in art. رسغ.) See 3, in that art.
