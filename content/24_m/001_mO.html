م مأ مأج

R. Q. 1. ⇒ مأمأ

مَأْمَأَتْ She (a ewe or she-goat or a gazelle) uttered continuously the cry مِئْ مِئْ, (Ḳ,) or (accord. to the Tes-heel) ماَءٓI مَآءِ: [and this is confirmed by a verse which I have cited voce تَخَوَّنَ:] (MF:) thus written in his Háshiyeh. (TA.)
