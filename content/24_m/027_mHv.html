محت محث محر

1. ⇒ محث

مَحَثَ شَيْئًا i. q. حَثَمَهُ. (L.)

مَحْثٌ

مَحْثٌ One who mixes with people, and eats and converses with them. (MF, from the Námoos of [the Mulla ʼAlee] El-Káree: [but SM expresses some doubt of its correctness, or whether it be correctly مخث]).
