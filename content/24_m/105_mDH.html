مض مضح مضر

1. ⇒ مضح

مَضَحَ عِرْضَهُ, aor. ـَ {يَمْضَحُ}; (inf. n. مَضْحٌ; TA;) andأَمْضَحَهُ↓, (inf. n. إِمْضَاحٌ; TA;) He disgraced, or dishonoured, him; blasted his reputation; (El-Umawee, Ṣ, Ḳ;) imputed to him, or accused him of, a vice, or fault, or the like. (TA.)

4. ⇒ امضح

see 1.
