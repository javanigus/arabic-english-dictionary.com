متن متى مث

مَتَى

مَتَى signifies When? and when used to denote a condition: see أَنَّى and أَيْنَ.

Root: متى - Entry: مَتَى ― Signification: A2

حَتَّى مَتَى Until when? how long? and also until the time when. See Freytag's Arab. Prov. i. 382.
