مهن مهو موأ

مَهْوٌ

مَهْوٌ, applied to a sword, Thin edged: see an ex. voce خَشِيبَةٌ.

Root: مهو - Entry: مَهْوٌ ― Signification: A2

سَلْحٌ مَهْوٌ Thin excrement. (Skr in Carm. Huds. p. 15.)

مَهًا

مَهًا † Front teeth (ثَغْرٌ) that are clean, white, and lustrous (having much مآء): so in a verse of El-Aạshà [cited voce رَفَّ]. (TA.)

مُمَهَّى

مُمَهَّى Beverage, or wine, (شَرَاب,) mixed with much water. (IAạr, in TA, art. حنذ.)
