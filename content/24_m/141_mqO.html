مغنطس مقأ مقت

مَاقِئٌ

مَاقِئٌ and مُوقِئٌ The posterior, or, as some say, anterior, part of the eye: (Ḳ:) or the inner angle of the eye, next the nose. (Ḳ, &c., art. مأق.) Mentioned by most of the lexicographers in art. مأق, q. v. (TA.)
