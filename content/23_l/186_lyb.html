ليأ ليب ليت

لَيَابٌ

لَيَابٌ A quantity of food less than what fills the mouth: (IAạr, Ḳ:) or a spoonful, that is chewed, or to be chewed. (Ḳ, and said to be on the authority of IAạr.) Mentioned by IM in art. لوب, and again in the present art. (TA.)
