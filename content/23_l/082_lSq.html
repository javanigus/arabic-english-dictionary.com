لصت لصق لط

3. ⇒ لاصق

لَاصَقَهُ He associated with him.

لَصِيقٌ

لَصِيقٌ An associate; an adherent.

مُلْصَقٌ

مُلْصَقٌ i. q. دَعِىٌّ (TA;) as alsoمُلَصَّقٌ↓ (TA in art. لسق) and مُلَسَّقٌ: (Ḳ, and TA in that art.:) or [a consociated alien;] one residing among a tribe of which he is not a member by lineage. (TA.)

مُلَصَّقٌ

مُلَصَّقٌ: see مُلْصَقٌ.
