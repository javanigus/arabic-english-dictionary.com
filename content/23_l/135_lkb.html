لكأ لكب لكث

مَلْكَبَةٌ

مَلْكَبَةٌ A she-camel compact in flesh: (Ḳ:) or abounding with fat, and compact in flesh. (AA, T.)

Root: لكب - Entry: مَلْكَبَةٌ ― Signification: A2

The conduct of a pimp: syn. قِيَادَةٌ (L.)
