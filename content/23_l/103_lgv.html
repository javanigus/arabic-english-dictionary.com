لغب لغث لغد

لَغِيثٌ

لَغِيثٌ i. q. غَلِيثٌ, (from which it is formed by transposition, TA,) in its two meanings: (Ḳ:) wheat mixed with barley; like بَغِيثٌ: (TA:) [and food mixed with poison, by which vultures are killed].

لُغَّاثٌ

لُغَّاثٌ [pl. of لَاغِثٌ] Sellers of wheat mixed with barley; as also بُغَّاثٌ. (L.)
