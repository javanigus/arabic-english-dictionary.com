لعط لعق لعل

1. ⇒ لعق

لَعِقَ He licked (Ṣ, Ḳ, TA) his fingers: (TA:) he ate a thing with his finger [by taking it up therewith]. (Mṣb.)

لَعُوقٌ

لَعُوقٌ A linctus.

مِلْعَقَةٌ

مِلْعَقَةٌ [A spoon; vulgo مَعْلَقَة;] a well-known instrument. (Mṣb.)
