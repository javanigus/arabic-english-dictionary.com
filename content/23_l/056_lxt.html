لخب لخت لخص

لَخْتٌ

لَخْتٌ Great, or big, in body: (Lth, Ṣgh, and some copies of the Ḳ:) or great, or big, and corpulent. (So in the other copies of the Ḳ.)

Root: لخت - Entry: لَخْتٌ ― Signification: A2

A woman in whom the division between the vagina and the rectum has been broken through; syn. مُفُضَاةٌ. (Ḳ.)

Root: لخت - Entry: لَخْتٌ ― Signification: A3

حَرٌّ سَخْتٌ لَخْتٌ Vehement, or intense, heat. (Lth, Ḳ.) [See also سَخْتٌ: and see لَحْتٌ.] Thought by ISd to be arabicized. (TA.)
