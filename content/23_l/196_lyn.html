ليل لين ليه

1. ⇒ لين ⇒ لان

لَانَ, inf. n. لِينٌ [not لَيْنٌ as in the CK] and لَياَنٌ, (T, Ṣ, M, Ḳ,) contr. of خَشُنَ; (Ṣ, Ḳ in art. خشن, and TḲ;) It was, or became, soft, as opposed to rough or harsh; smooth; plain; without asperities; fine to the touch; delicate; tender; supple; lithe; limber; pliant; pliable; flexible; ductile; malleable; soft, or flabby; lax: and he was, or became, soft; tender; pliant; gentle; bland; or mild. Hence لان جَانِبُهُ: see لَيِّنٌ.

Root: لين - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A2

لَانَ بَطْنُهُ His bowels became relaxed.

Root: لين - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A3

لَانَ He relented.

3. ⇒ لاين

لَايَنَهُ بِالقَوْلِ He soothed, coaxed, or wheedled, him with words. (L, art. مسح.)

Root: لين - Entry: 3. ― Signification: A2

لَايَنَهُ [He acted gently towards him; (M, Ḳ;) treated him with gentleness, or blandishment; soothed him; coaxed him; wheedled him;] i. q. دَارَاهُ. (Ṣ, M, Mṣb, voce داراه.)

Root: لين - Entry: 3. ― Signification: A3

لَايَنَهُ He was soft, tender, gentle, bland, or mild, towards him. (M, Ḳ.)

4. ⇒ الين ⇒ الان

أَلَانَ بَطْنَهُ [It relaxed his bowels]; said of medicine. (Ḳ in art. سهل.)

10. ⇒ استلين ⇒ استلان

اِسْتَلَانَ: see its contr. استخشن.

لَانَ

لَانَ for الْآنَ: see the latter in art. اين.

لِينُ

لِينُ العَيْشِ Softness, delicateness, or easiness, of life.

لِينَةٌ

لِينَةٌ applied to a palm-tree: see art. لون; and see عَجْوَةٌ.

لَيِّنٌ

عَيْشٌ لَيِّنٌ Soft, delicate, or easy, life.

Root: لين - Entry: لَيِّنٌ ― Signification: A2

لَيِّنُ الجَانِبِ: see جَانِبٌ.

Root: لين - Entry: لَيِّنٌ ― Signification: A3

لَيِّنُ الأَعْطَافِ;, &c.: see art. عطف.

مُلَيِّنٌ

مُلَيِّنٌ A lenitive, or laxative, medicine.
