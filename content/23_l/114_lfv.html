لفت لفث لفج

10. ⇒ استلفث

استلفث مَا عِنْدَهُ He elicited, and exhausted, (إِسْتَنْبَطَ وَٱسْتَقْصَى,) what [information, &c.] he had, or possessed. (Ḳ.)

Root: لفث - Entry: 10. ― Signification: A2

استلفث الرِّعْىَ He consumed the whole of the pasture, leaving nothing of it. (Ḳ, TA.)

Root: لفث - Entry: 10. ― Signification: A3

استلفث حَاجَتَهُ He accomplished his want. (Ḳ.)

Root: لفث - Entry: 10. ― Signification: A4

استلفث الخَبَرَ He concealed the news. (Ḳ.)

أَلْفَثُ

أَلْفَثُ Stupid; foolish; of little sense: (Ḳ:) like أَلْفَتُ. (TA.)
