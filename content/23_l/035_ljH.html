لجب لجح لجد

لُجْحٌ

لُجْحٌ A thing in the lower part of a well, and of a valley, like what is called a دَحْل: (Ṣ, Ḳ:) or, in the lower part of a well, and of a mountain, like a نَقْب: (L:) originally لُحْجٌ, from which it is formed by transposition: (T:) pl. أَلْجَاحٌ. (L.)
