لهلأ لهم لهو

4. ⇒ الهم

أَلْهَمَهُ ٱللّٰهُ لِلْخَيْرِ [God directed him by inspiration to that which was good, or to prosperity] (TA, art. وفق)

Root: لهم - Entry: 4. ― Signification: A2

أَلْهَمَهُ إِيَّاهُ He suggested it to him; (in the order of the words, he suggested to him it;) he put it into his mind.

8. ⇒ التهم

اِلْتَهَمَ He gulped.

لَاهُمَّ

لَاهُمَّ and ٱللّٰهُمَّ: see ٱللّٰهُ.
