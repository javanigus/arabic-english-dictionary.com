لذ لذب لذع

1. ⇒ لذب

لَذَبَ, aor. ـُ {يَلْذُبُ}, inf. n. لُذُوبٌ; andلَاذَبَ↓; He remained, stayed, abode, or dwelt, in a place: (Ḳ:) or correctly written with د, unpointed: but IDrd doubts whether correctly with ذ or with د. (TA.) See also لَزَبَ.

3. ⇒ لاذب

see 1.
