لوى ليأ ليب

4. ⇒ اليأ

اليأت النَّاقَةُ The she-camel was slow. (Ḳ.)

لِيَآءٌ

لِيَآءٌ A kind of white grain, resembling the حِمِّص [or cicer arietinum], (Ḳ,) intensely white, which is eaten. (AḤn says, I know not whether it [the plant] have, or have not, قطنيّة [q. v.]. TA.)
