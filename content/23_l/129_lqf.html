لقط لقف لقم

1. ⇒ لقف

لَقِفَهُ He seized it, or took it quickly; (Ṣ, M, Ḳ;) namely, a thing thrown to him with the hand, or said to him. (M, TA.) See also last sentence of 1 in art. حنك.

5. ⇒ تلقّف

تَلَقَّفَهُ مِنْ فَمِهِ He caught it and retained it quickly, [i. e., what he said,] from his mouth. (TA.)
