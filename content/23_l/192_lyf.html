ليغ ليف ليق

لِيفٌ

لِيفٌ [The membranous fibres that grow at the base of the branches of the palm-tree:] the best sort is the ليف of the cocoa-nut. (TA.) See شَرِيعٌ.

Root: ليف - Entry: لِيفٌ ― Signification: A2

لِيفٌ is used by Ibn-Mukbil as meaning † A she-camel's tail. (TA in arts. شذب and شمل.)
