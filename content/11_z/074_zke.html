زكو زكى زل

1. ⇒ زكى

زَكِىَ, (Ḳ,) aor. يَزْكَى, inf. n. زَكَاةٌ, i. q. زَكَا, aor. يَزْكُو, as meaning It increased, or augmented; (Lḥ, ISd, Ḳ,* TA;) and produced fruit: (TA:) andتزكّى↓ signifies the same. (Ḳ.)

Root: زكى - Entry: 1. ＝ Dissociation: B

Also He thirsted. (Th, Ḳ.)

5. ⇒ تزكّى

see above {1}; and see art. زكو.
