زمت زمخ زمر

1. ⇒ زمخ

زَمَخَ, (Ṣ, Ḳ,) or زَمَخَ بِأَنْفِهِ, (L,) aor. ـَ {يَزْمَخُ}, (Ḳ,) inf. n. زَمْخٌ, (L,) He exalted, or magnified, himself; was proud; (Ṣ, L, Ḳ;) behaved proudly, haughtily, or vainly; (Ṣ, L;) elevated his nose, from pride; (L;) i. q. شَمَخَ, or شَمَخَ بِأَنْفِهِ. (TA.)

زَمَخٌ

زَمَخٌ: see زَمُوخٌ, in two places.

زِمْخٌ

زِمْخٌ or زِمَخٌ The tree called سُمَّاق; as also ظِمْخٌ or ظِمَخٌ. (TA in art. ظمخ; but there written without any syll. signs.)

زَمُوخٌ

عُقْبَةٌ زَمُوخٌ [in the CK عَقَبَةٌ] (JK, A, Ḳ) andزَمَخٌ↓ (Ḳ) ‡ [A stage of a journey] far-extending, (Ḳ,) hard, or difficult. (AZ, IAạr, JK, Ḳ.) One says, سَارَ عُقْبَةً زَمُوخًا ‡ [He journeyed a long and hard stage]. (A.)

Root: زمخ - Entry: زَمُوخٌ ― Signification: A2

And نِيَّةٌ زَمُوخٌ (A, and L in art. شمخ) andزَمَخٌ↓, like شَمُوخٌ and شَمَخٌ, (L in that art.,) ‡ A distant, far-reaching, or faraiming, intention, purpose, or design. (A, and L ubi suprà.)

زَامِخٌ

زَامِخٌ i. q. شَامِخٌ [Proud,, &c.]; (Ṣ, Ḳ;) or شَامِخٌ بِأَنْفِهِ [elevating his nose, from pride]: (A, L:) [pl. زُمَّخٌ.]

Root: زمخ - Entry: زَامِخٌ ― Signification: A2

أُنُوفٌ زُمَّخٌ i. q. شُمَّخٌ [Noses elevated, from pride]. (Ṣ, A.)

Root: زمخ - Entry: زَامِخٌ ― Signification: A3

[Hence,] جِبَالٌ لَهَا أُنُوفٌ زُمَّخٌ, (A, TA,) i. e. طِوَالٌ ‡ [Mountains having tall, or long, prominences]. (TA.)

Root: زمخ - Entry: زَامِخٌ ― Signification: A4

And كَيْلٌ زَامِخٌ ‡ Full measure. (JK, A, Ḳ.)
