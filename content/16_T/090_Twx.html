طوح طوخ طود

1. ⇒ طوخ ⇒ طاخ

طَاخَهُ, (Ḳ,) aor. يَطُوخُهُ, (TA,) inf. n. طَوْخٌ; (Ḳ;) as also طَاخَهُ, aor. يَطِيخُهُ, inf. n. طَيْخٌ; which is the more common; (TA;) He charged, reproached, or upbraided, him with something bad, evil, abominable, or foul, either said or done. (Ḳ.) [See also art. طيخ.]
