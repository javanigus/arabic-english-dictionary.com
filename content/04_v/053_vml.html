1. ⇒ ثمل

ثَمَلَ, [aor., app., ـِ and ـُ,] inf. n. ثَمْلٌ, It (water) remained in a watering-trough or tank. (Mṣb.)

Root: ثمل - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A2
