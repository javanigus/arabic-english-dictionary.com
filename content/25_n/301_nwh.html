نون نوه نوى

1. ⇒ نوه ⇒ ناه

نَاهَ بِهِ (Mṣb) andنَوَّهَ↓ بِهِ (Ṣ, Mgh, Mṣb, Ḳ) He rendered famous (Ṣ, Mgh, Mṣb, Ḳ *) and magnified; (Mṣb;) and rendered notorious; (Mgh;) it, (Ṣ, Mṣb,) namely a person's name, (Ṣ,) or a thing, (Mṣb,) or him. (Mgh.)

2. ⇒ نوّه

نَوَّهَ see 1.
