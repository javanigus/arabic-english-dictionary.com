نئف ناى نب

1. ⇒ ناى

نَأَى بِهِ [He, or it, removed him; put or placed him at a distance, away, or far away.] (Ṣ, art. طرح.)

Root: ناى - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A2

نَأَى He shrank from a thing: see an ex. in a verse cited voce دَفٌّ.

4. ⇒ اناى

أَنْآهُ: see آنَاهُ in art. انى.

10. ⇒ استناى

اِسْتَنْأَى i. q. اِسْتَنَاءَ q. v. in art. نوأ.

نُؤْىٌ

نُؤْىٌ A trench dug round a tent, (Ṣ, Ḳ,) or a barrier [raised] around it, (T, IB, TA,) to prevent the rain-water from entering it, (Ṣ,) or keep off a torrent. (Ḳ.)
