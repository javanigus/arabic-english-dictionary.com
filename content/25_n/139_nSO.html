نص نصأ نصب

1. ⇒ نصأ

نَصَأَهُ, aor. ـَ {يَنْصَأُ}, He took him by the نَاصِيَة [or forelock]: (Ḳ:) from نَاصِيَةٌ: and therefore regarded by some as improperly mentioned in the Ḳ in this art. (MF.)

Root: نصأ - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A2

نَصَأَ, aor. ـَ {يَنْصَأُ}, (Ṣ, Ḳ,) inf. n. نَصْءٌ, (TA,) He chid, (a camel, TA, or she-camel, Ṣ); syn. زَجَرَ. (AZ, Ṣ, Ḳ.)

Root: نصأ - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A3

نَصَأَ, (Ṣ, Ḳ,) inf. n. as above, (TA,) He raised, or elevated, a thing: (Ks, AA, Ṣ, Ḳ:) dial. form of نَصَّ. (Ṣ.)
