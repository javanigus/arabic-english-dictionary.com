نرج نرجس نرد

نَرْجِسٌ

نَرْجِسٌ and نِرْجِسٌ [The Narcissus]: see art. رجس. The former is mentioned by ISd in art. رجس: the latter, in the present art. (TA.)

Supplement:

نَرْجِسِيَّةٌ

دَابَّةٌ نَرْجِسِيَّةٌ A beast of carriage whose whiteness inclines to yellowness [like the narcissus]. (TA, art. قرطس.)
