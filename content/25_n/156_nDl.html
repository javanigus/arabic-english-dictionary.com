نضر نضل نضو

1. ⇒ نضل

نَضَلَهُ He overcame him, or surpassed him, in shooting. (Ṣ, Mṣb, Ḳ.)

3. ⇒ ناضل

نَاضَلَهُ He vied, competed, or contended for superiority, with him in shooting. (Ṣ, Mṣb, Ḳ.)

Root: نضل - Entry: 3. ― Signification: A2

نَاضَلَهُ, inf. n. تِنْصَالٌ: see بَيَّنَهَ.

Root: نضل - Entry: 3. ― Signification: A3

نَاضَل عَنْهُ ‡ He defended him, pleaded in defence of him, or repelled from him; (Ḳ, TA;) spoke in his defence, excusing him; (Ṣ, TA;) defended him,, &c., as above; (Ṣ;) contended, or pleaded, in his defence; (TA;) defended him; and contended in his defence. (Mṣb.)
