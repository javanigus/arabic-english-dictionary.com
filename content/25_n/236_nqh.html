نقم نقه نقى

1. ⇒ نقه

نَقَهَ He recovered, but not completely, his health and strength: (TA:) or he became convalescent; or sound, or healthy; at the close of his disease: (Ṣ:) or sound, or healthy, but was yet weak. (Ḳ.) See بَرِئَ.

نَقاَهَةٌ

نَقاَهَةٌ [Convalescence;] the slight degree of health that immediately succeeds sickness. (TA, art. برأ.)
