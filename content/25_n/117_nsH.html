نسج نسح نسخ

1. ⇒ نسح

نَسَحَ, aor. ـَ {يَنْسَحُ}, (inf. n. نَسْحٌ, Ṣ,) He dispersed and removed dust; syn. أَذْرَى. (Ḳ, and some copies of the Ṣ.)

Root: نسح - Entry: 1. ＝ Dissociation: B

نَسِحَ, aor. ـَ {يَنْسَحُ}, (inf. n. نَسَحٌ, Ṣ,) He coveted; syn. طَمِعَ. (Ḳ, and some copies of the Ṣ.)

مِنْسَاحٌ

مِنْسَاحٌ A thing with which dust is dispersed and removed. (Ḳ.)
