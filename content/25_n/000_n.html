ن

Arabic Letter ن

The twenty-fifth letter of the alphabet; called نُونٌ: it is one of the class termed ذَلْقِيَّةٌ [or liquids]; and is a letter of augmentation.

Root: ن - Entry: ن ― Signification: A2

نّ with teshdeed, and preceded by a fat-hah, is sometimes redundantly affixed to a word at the end of a verse: see an ex. voce تَوٌّ.

Root: ن - Entry: ن ― Signification: A3

ن, the sign of the dual, with damm, in one dial., instead of kesr, see خَشِيفٌ.

Root: ن - Entry: ن ― Signification: A4

See also صَادٌ.

Root: ن - Entry: ن ― Signification: A5

As a numeral, it denotes fifty.
