نيب نيت نير

1. ⇒ نيت ⇒ نات

نَاتَ, aor. يَنِيتُ, inf. n. نَيْتٌ; as also نَاتَ, aor. يَنُوتُ, inf. n. نَوْتٌ; (L, Ḳ;) He (a man) moved from side to side in walking (L, art نوت:) or he so moved by reason of weakness, or infirmity: (Ḳ:) or, by reason of drowsiness. (L, art. نوت, q. v.)
