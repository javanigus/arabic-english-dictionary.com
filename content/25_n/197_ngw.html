نغم نغو نفأ

3. ⇒ ناغو ⇒ ناغى

نَاغَاهُ He interchanged speech with him, each of them addressing the other with a word or saying: (TA:) نَغَيْتُ إِلَيْهِ نَغْيَةٌ signifies I addressed to him a word or saying: and المُنَاغَاةُ signifies المُوَاجَهَةُ. (JK.)
