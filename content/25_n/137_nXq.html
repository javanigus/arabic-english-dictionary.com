5. ⇒ تنشّق

تَنَشَّقَ see 10.

10. ⇒ استنشق

اِسْتَنْشَقَ الرِّيحَ † [He snuffed the wind]: (TA, art. مخر:) he snuffed, scented, or smelt, the wind; as alsoتَنَشَّقَهَا↓. (Mṣb.) See 10 in art. شم.

نُشَاقَةٌ

نُشَاقَةٌ What is taken [or ladled out], while hot, from a cooking-pot. (TA.)
