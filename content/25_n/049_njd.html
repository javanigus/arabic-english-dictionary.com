A wine-vessel: (Ḳ:) any vessel into which wine is put, (AʼObeyd, Ṣ, Ḳ,*) such as a بَاطِيَة, (L,) or a جَفْنَة &c.: (AʼObeyd, Ṣ, L:) or a wine-cup, or a cup of wine; syn. كَأْسٌ: (L:) or a vessel in which wine is cleared; (A;) a clarifier, or strainer for wine; syn. رَاوُوقٌ; (Lth, L;) which last is the meaning that most assign to the word. (TA.)

Root: نجد - Entry: نَاجُودٌ ― Signification: A3

Saffron. (Aṣ, L, Ḳ.)

Root: نجد - Entry: نَاجُودٌ ― Signification: A4

Blood. (Aṣ, L, Ḳ.)

مِنْجَدٌ

مِنْجَدٌ A small mountain (Ḳ, [in the CK, for جُبَيْل is put حُبَيْل,]) overlooking a valley. (TA.)

Root: نجد - Entry: مِنْجَدٌ ― Signification: A2

مِنْجَدٌ A kind of ornament, (L, Ḳ,) worn by women, (L,) adorned with gems, or jewels, (L, Ḳ,) one over another: (L:) a necklace of pearls and gold, or of cloves, a span in breadth, extending from the neck to the part beneath the breasts, and lying upon the place of the نِجَاد; (L, Ḳ;) i. e. of the نجاد of a man's sword: from نَجَّدَ البَيْتَ: (L:) pl. مَنَاجِدُ. (L, Ḳ.)

مِنْجَدَةٌ

مِنْجَدَةٌ A light staff or stick with which a beast of carriage is urged on. (Ḳ.)

Root: نجد - Entry: مِنْجَدَةٌ ― Signification: A2

A stick, or wooden instrument, with which wool is teased, or separated, i. q. مِنْدَفٌ (?) (TA,) and with which the حَقِيبَة of a camel's saddle is stuffed. (Ḳ, TA.)

مِنْجَادٌ

مِنْجَادٌ A man who aids, or assists, much or well. (Lḥ, L.)

مَنْجُودٌ

مُنَجَّدٌ

مُنَجَّدٌ, as also مُنَجَّذٌ, (Ṣ, L,) which latter is the more approved, (L,) A man tried and strengthened by experience; expert, or experienced; (Ṣ, L, Ḳ;) who has had experience in affairs, and has estimated and understood them, and become well informed. (L.)
