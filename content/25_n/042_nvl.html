نثر نثل نثى

1. ⇒ نثل

نَثَلٌ He cleansed an intestine: see مُبْعَرٌ.

نَثُولٌ

نَثُولٌ A lean, or emaciated, woman. (IAạr, TA, art. جمل.)

نَثِيلٌ

نَثِيلٌ The dust, or earth, of the foundations of a house. (TA, art. ثوب.)

نَثِيلَةُ

نَثِيلَةُ بِئْرٍ The earth that is around a well, that is seen from afar. (Ṣ, art. جبو.)
