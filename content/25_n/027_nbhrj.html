نبه نبهرج نبى

نَبَهْرَجٌ

نَبَهْرَجٌ i. q. بَهْرَجٌ, q. v. (TA). [The place in which it is mentioned in the Ḳ shows that F regards the ن as a radical letter; and though it is said in the TA that its being so is doubtful, he is right accord. to those who hold that every letter of an arabicized word is to be regarded as a radical if it, or a letter for which it is substituted, is found in the original.]
