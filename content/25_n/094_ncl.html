نذر نذل نرب

1. ⇒ نذل

نَذُلَ, inf. n. نَذَالَةٌ, He was, or became, low, base, vile, ignoble, mean, sordid, or contemptible, (Ṣ, Ḳ,) in all his circumstances. (Ḳ.)

نَذْلٌ

نَذْلٌ A man vile, or mean, and despised in all his circumstances. (Ḳ.)
