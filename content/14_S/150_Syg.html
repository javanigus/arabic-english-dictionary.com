صيع صيغ صيف

2. ⇒ صيّغ

صيّغ طَعَامَهُ, (ISh, O, Ḳ,) inf. n. تَصْيِيغٌ (Ḳ,) He soaked his food in sauce, or seasoning: (ISh, O, Ḳ:) and صيّغهُ بِالسَّمْنِ He soaked it with clarified butter. (ISh, O.) [صَبَغَهُ has a similar meaning, and is better known.]

صِيغَةٌ

صِيغَةٌ, see art. صوغ.

Other words in which ى is substituted for و, see in art. صوغ.
