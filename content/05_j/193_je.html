جوى جى جيأ

3. ⇒ جاىّ

جَايَاهُ, inf. n. مُجَايَاةٌ, He faced him, fronted him, was opposite to him, or was over against him: a dial. var. of جَايَأَهُ. (IAạr, Ḳ.) You say also, مَرَّ بِى مُجَايَاةً He passed by me being in front, or opposite. (TA.)
