جث جثل جثم

1. ⇒ جثل

جَثُلَ, aor. ـُ {يَجْثُلُ}; (Lth, Mṣb, Ḳ;) and جَثِلَ, aor. ـَ {يَجْثَلُ}; (Ḳ;) inf. n. جُثُولَةٌ and جَثَالَةٌ; (Mṣb, Ḳ;) both of the former verb; (Mṣb, TA;) [but both mentioned in the Ṣ as simple substs.;] It was, or became, such as is termed جَثْلٌ, explained below. (Mṣb, Ḳ.)

Q. Q. 4. ⇒ اِجْثَأَلَّ

اِجْثَأَلَّ He (a bird) ruffled his feathers (Ṣ, Ḳ) by reason of the cold. (TA.)

Root: جثل - Entry: Q. Q. 4. ― Signification: A2

† He (a man) became angry, and prepared himself for fighting. (Ṣ, Ḳ.)

Root: جثل - Entry: Q. Q. 4. ― Signification: A3

† It (a plant, or herbage,) became tall, and tangled, or luxuriant, or abundant and dense: (Z, Ḳ:) or became tall (اِهْتَزَّ, q. v.), and such as might be grasped with the hand. (AZ, Ṣ, Ḳ.)

Root: جثل - Entry: Q. Q. 4. ― Signification: A4

It (the plumage of a bird) became ruffled. (Ḳ.)

جَثْلٌ

جَثْلٌ (AZ, Ṣ, Mṣb, Ḳ) andجَثِيلٌ↓ (Ḳ) A thing, (Mṣb,) or hair, (AZ, Ṣ, Ḳ,) and ‡ trees (شَجَرٌ), (Ḳ, TA,) much, or abundant, (AZ, Ṣ, Mṣb, Ḳ,) and thick, or abundant, (AZ, Ṣ, Mṣb, Ḳ,) and thick, or coarse, or rough, (Mṣb,) or tangled, or luxuriant and dense, (Ḳ,) and soft: (TA:) or thick, or coarse, or rough, and short: or dense and black: (Ḳ:) or the blackest hair: or the thickest, or coarsest, or roughest: (Lth, TA:) or anything large, big, or bulky, and dense, and tangled, or luxuriant. (Ḳ.) You say لِحْيَةٌ An abundant, thick, or coarse, or rough, beard. (Mṣb:) or a thick, or dense, beard. (TA.) And نَاصِيَةٌ جَثْلَةٌ A horse's forelock moderate in respect of quantity and length: such is approved. (Ṣ.) And شَجَرَةٌ جَثْلَةٌ ‡ A many-leaved, big, tree. (Ṣ, Ḳ, TA.)

Root: جثل - Entry: جَثْلٌ ＝ Dissociation: B

جَثْلٌ A species of ant, large and black; as also جَفْلٌ: (IDrd, TA:) or جَثْلَةٌ signifies a black ant: (Ṣ:) or a large and: and جَثْلٌ is its pl. [or coll. gen. n.]. (Ḳ.)

جَثَيلٌ

جَثَيلٌ: see جَثْلٌ.

جُثَالَةٌ

جُثَالَةٌ Leaves that have become gradually scattered, or strewn. (Ḳ.)

مُجْثَئِلٌّ

مُجْثَئِلٌّ Brood, or wide. (Ḳ.)

Root: جثل - Entry: مُجْثَئِلٌّ ― Signification: A2

Standing erect. (Ṣ, Ḳ.)
