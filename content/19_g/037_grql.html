غرقد غرقل غرل

Q. 1. ⇒ غرقل

غَرْقَلَتِ البَيْضَةُ The egg became rotten; (Ṣ, O;) the interior of the egg became corrupt: and thus غَرْقَلَ signifies said of the بِطِّيخ [i. e. melon, or water-melon]: (Ḳ:) or, said of the بطّيخ, it became hard. (O.)

Root: غرقل - Entry: Q. 1. ＝ Dissociation: B

And غَرْقَلَ He poured water upon his head at once; (IAạr, O, Ḳ, TA;) inf. n. غَرْقَلَةٌ. (TA.)

غِرْقِلٌ

غِرْقِلٌ The white of an egg; as alsoغِرْقِيلٌ↓. (Az, TA.)
