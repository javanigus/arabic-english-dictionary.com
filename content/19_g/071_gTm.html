غطل غطم غطمط

غِطَمٌّ

غِطَمٌّ A great sea, (Ṣ, Ḳ, TA,) abundant in water; (Ṣ, TA;) andغِطْيَمٌّ↓, (Ḳ, TA,) like قِرْشَبُّ, (TA,) [in the CK غَطِيْم,] signifies the same; as alsoغَطَمْطَمٌ↓: (Ḳ, TA:) one says بَحْرٌ غِطَمٌّ. (Ṣ.)

Root: غطم - Entry: غِطَمٌّ ― Signification: A2

And † A man large (Ṣ, Ḳ) in disposition (Ṣ) or dispositions; (Ḳ;) [i. e.] liberal, or bountiful: (TA:) one says رَجُلٌ غِطَمٌّ. (Ṣ.)

Root: غطم - Entry: غِطَمٌّ ― Signification: A3

And A numerous company, or collective body: (Ḳ, TA:) one says جَمْعٌ غِطَمٌّ. (Ṣ, TA.) Andعَدَدٌ غِطْيَمٌّ↓, like قِرْشَبٌّ, A large number. (TA.) [See also غُطَامِطٌ, in art. غطمط.]

غِطْيَمٌّ

غِطْيَمٌّ: see the preceding paragraph, in two places.

غَطَمُطَمٌ

غَطَمُطَمٌ: see the first paragraph (غِطَمٌّ).

غَيْطَمٌّ

غَيْطَمٌّ Thick milk. (Ḳ, TA.)
