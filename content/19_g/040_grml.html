غرم غرمل غرنق

غُرْمُولٌ

غُرْمُولٌ The penis, (Ṣ, O, Ḳ,) in an absolute sense: (TA:) or a large and flaccid penis before its prepuce is cut off; (Ḳ;) thus says AZ: (TA:) or it is said to be of a solid-hoofed animal: but mention is made in a trad. of the غَرَامِيل of men [in relation to whom, however, it may perhaps be used in this instance by way of comparison]. (TA.)
