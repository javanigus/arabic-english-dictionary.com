رقم رقو رقى

1. ⇒ رقو ⇒ رقى

رَقَا, aor. يَرْقُو, inf. n. رَقْوٌ, said of a bird, It rose, or rose high, in its flight. (Mṣb, TA.)

تَرْقُوَةٌ

تَرْقُوَةٌ, mentioned in this art. in the Ḳ, as well as in art. ترق: see the latter art.
