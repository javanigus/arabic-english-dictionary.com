رند رنز رنق

رُنْزٌ

رُنْزٌ i. q. أَرُزٌّ [and رُزٌّ, i. e. Rice]; (Ṣ, Ḳ; [incorrectly said in the TA to be omitted by J;]) in the dial. of ʼAbd-el-Keys: (ISd, TA:) as though the first ز were changed into ن, (Ṣ, TA,) as ج in إِنْجَاصٌ for إِجَّاصٌ. (TA.) [See حَظٌّ.]
