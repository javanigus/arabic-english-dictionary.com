ردن ردو ردى

1. ⇒ ردو ⇒ ردى

رَدَا aor. يَرْدِو said of a horse: see 1 in art. ردى.

Root: ردو - Entry: 1. ― Signification: A2

And رَدَاهُ بِحَجَرٍ, aor. يَرْدُوهُ, inf. n. رَدْوٌ: see 1 in art. ردى.

3. ⇒ رادو ⇒ رادى

راداهُ, said to be formed by transposition from رَاوَدَهُ, [and therefore it should properly be mentioned in this art.,] is explained in art. ردى.
